# 看图作文 <DifficultyBadge level="B2" />

> 看图作文近年考频越来越高。套路固定：**描述图片 → 揭示寓意 → 发表评论**。

## 万能结构

```
第一段：描述图片内容（1-2句客观描述）
第二段：揭示寓意 + 分析原因（为什么重要）
第三段：个人观点 + 行动号召
```

---

## 第一段：描述图片

<ExampleSentence
  en="As is vividly depicted in the picture, a young man is sitting in front of a computer, ignoring the beautiful scenery outside the window."
  cn="如图所示，一个年轻人坐在电脑前，忽视了窗外的美景。"
  highlights={["As is vividly depicted in the picture"]}
  note="万能开头句 — 所有看图作文都能用"
/>

**更多开头模板：**

| 模板 | 适用场景 |
|------|---------|
| As is shown in the picture,... | 通用 |
| The picture illustrates a scene where... | 场景类 |
| What a thought-provoking picture! | 发人深省的图片 |
| The cartoon humorously shows that... | 漫画/幽默类 |

<ExampleSentence
  en="The picture illustrates a scene where two people are climbing a mountain — one is about to give up halfway, while the other persists and reaches the top."
  cn="图片描绘了两个人爬山的场景——一个人在半途准备放弃，另一个则坚持到达了山顶。"
  highlights={["The picture illustrates a scene where"]}
/>

## 第二段：揭示寓意

<ExampleSentence
  en="The picture conveys a profound message that perseverance is the key to success."
  cn="这幅图传达了一个深刻的信息：坚持是成功的关键。"
  highlights={["conveys a profound message that"]}
  note="点明寓意的核心句型"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="The reason behind this phenomenon is that many people lack the determination to overcome difficulties."
  cn="这种现象背后的原因是许多人缺乏克服困难的决心。"
  highlights={["The reason behind this phenomenon is that"]}
/>

**常用寓意句型：**

| 模板 | 中文 |
|------|------|
| The picture is meant to convey that... | 这幅图意在传达…… |
| It reflects a common phenomenon that... | 它反映了一个普遍现象…… |
| The message is clear:... | 信息很明确：…… |

---

## 第三段：评论总结

<ExampleSentence
  en="From my perspective, we should cultivate the habit of perseverance in our daily lives."
  cn="在我看来，我们应该在日常生活中培养坚持的习惯。"
  highlights={["From my perspective", "cultivate the habit of"]}
/>

<ExampleSentence

  highlights={["Only by", "can we"]}
  note="倒装升华句 — 结尾加分利器"

## 范文示例

**题目：** 下图描绘了一棵树在不同季节的变化，旁边写着 "成长需要时间"。

:::info 📖 范文
As is vividly depicted in the picture, a tree undergoes dramatic changes across four seasons — from a bare trunk in winter to a lush, fruit-bearing tree in autumn. Beside it, a caption reads: "Growth takes time."

The picture conveys a profound message that success and personal growth require patience and persistence. In today's fast-paced society, many young people expect instant results and become frustrated when progress seems slow. However, just like a tree that needs years to bear fruit, meaningful achievements take time to develop. Whether it is learning a skill, building a career, or developing a relationship, rushing the process often leads to disappointment.

From my perspective, we should embrace the journey of growth rather than focusing solely on the destination. As the saying goes, "Rome was not built in a day." Only by maintaining patience and consistent effort can we eventually reap the rewards of our hard work.
:::

## 实战练习

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="看图作文的第一段应该做什么？"
  options={["直接发表个人观点", "客观描述图片内容", "引用名人名言", "分析社会问题"]}
  answer="客观描述图片内容"
  explanation="第一段应客观描述图片中看到的内容（人物、场景、动作），不要急于评论。第二段再揭示寓意。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="As is vividly _____ in the picture, a young man is reading a book.（描绘）"
  answer="depicted"
  explanation="As is vividly depicted in the picture 是看图作文最经典的开头句式。depicted 意为'描绘、刻画'。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="以下哪个句型最适合用在看图作文的结尾升华？"
  options={["As is shown in the picture,...", "The picture illustrates...", "Only by taking action can we make a difference.", "I think it is good."]}
  answer="Only by taking action can we make a difference."
  explanation="Only by... can we... 是倒装升华句式，用在结尾能体现语言水平，是阅卷老师青睐的高级句式。"
/>
