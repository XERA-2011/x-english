# 图表描述写作 <DifficultyBadge level="B2" />

> 图表作文的核心：**改写题目 → 概述趋势 → 描述数据 → 总结**。记住趋势词汇就够了。

## 万能结构

```
第一段：改写题目 + 概述主要趋势
第二段：描述主要特征/趋势 (高的、增长的)
第三段：描述次要特征/趋势 (低的、下降的)
第四段：总结（可选）
```

---

## 趋势词汇速查

### 📈 上升

| 动词 | 程度 |
|------|------|
| increase / rise / grow | 中性 |
| surge / soar | 大幅 |
| climb gradually | 小幅 |

**程度副词：** significantly（大幅）/ steadily（稳步）/ slightly（小幅）

<ExampleSentence
  en="The number of online shoppers increased dramatically from 100 million to 500 million."
  cn="网购用户数量从1亿大幅增长到5亿。"
  highlights={["increased dramatically", "from...to..."]}
/>

### 📉 下降

| 动词 | 程度 |
|------|------|
| decrease / decline / fall | 中性 |
| plunge / plummet | 大幅 |
| dip slightly | 小幅 |

<ExampleSentence
  en="The unemployment rate declined steadily from 8% to 4%."
  cn="失业率从8%稳步下降到4%。"
  highlights={["declined steadily"]}
/>

### ➡️ 波动 & 稳定

| 动词 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| fluctuate | 波动 |
| remain stable | 保持稳定 |
| level off | 趋于平稳 |

## 常用句型

**改写题目：**
> The bar chart **illustrates** the proportion of... / The line graph **depicts** the trend of...

**概述：**

<ExampleSentence
  en="Overall, there was a significant increase in the number of students studying abroad."
  cn="总体而言，留学生数量显著增加。"
  highlights={["Overall", "significant increase"]}
/>

**比较：**

<ExampleSentence
  en="China had the largest number of international students, followed by India and South Korea."
  cn="中国的留学生数量最多，其次是印度和韩国。"
  highlights={["the largest", "followed by"]}
/>

**比例：**

<ExampleSentence
  en="Education accounted for the largest proportion of government spending, at 30%."
  cn="教育占政府支出的最大比例，为30%。"
  highlights={["accounted for", "at 30%"]}
/>

## 范文示例

**题目：** 不同年龄段的互联网使用率（16-24: 98%, 25-34: 95%, 45-54: 75%, 65+: 40%）

:::info 📖 范文
The bar chart illustrates the proportion of internet users across various age groups in 2020. Overall, internet usage decreases with age.

Young adults aged 16-24 had the highest internet usage rate at 98%, closely followed by the 25-34 age group at 95%. The 35-44 age group also showed high usage at 88%.

In contrast, internet adoption was considerably lower among older groups. Only 60% of those aged 55-64 used the internet, and merely 40% of those over 65 were online.

In conclusion, the data reveals a strong correlation between age and internet usage, highlighting the need to improve digital literacy among senior citizens.
:::

## 实战练习

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="描述过去的数据变化时，应该使用什么时态？"
  options={["一般现在时", "一般过去时", "现在完成时", "一般将来时"]}
  answer="一般过去时"
  explanation="描述过去特定时间段的数据用一般过去时（increased, decreased）。描述图表本身用现在时（The chart shows...）。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="以下哪个词最适合描述「小幅上升」？"
  options={["surged", "soared", "rose slightly", "plunged"]}
  answer="rose slightly"
  explanation="rose slightly 表示小幅上升。surged/soared 表示大幅上升，plunged 表示大幅下降。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="Education _____ for the largest proportion of government spending.（占据）"
  answer="accounted"
  explanation="account for 是描述比例时的固定搭配，意为'占（比例）'。"
/>
