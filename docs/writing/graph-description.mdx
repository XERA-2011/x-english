# 图表描述写作 Graph Description <DifficultyBadge level="B2" />

> 图表描述要求客观准确地描述数据趋势和特征，是学术写作的重要技能。

## 一、图表类型

| 图表类型 | 英文名称 | 用途 |
|---------|---------|------|
| 柱状图 | Bar Chart | 比较不同类别的数据 |
| 折线图 | Line Graph | 显示数据随时间的变化趋势 |
| 饼图 | Pie Chart | 显示各部分占整体的比例 |
| 表格 | Table | 呈现详细的数值数据 |
| 流程图 | Flow Chart | 展示过程或步骤 |

---

## 二、写作结构

### 四段式结构（推荐）

```
第一段：改写题目 + 概述（Overview）
第二段：描述主要特征/趋势1
第三段：描述主要特征/趋势2
第四段：总结（可选）
```

### 三段式结构

```
第一段：改写题目
第二段：概述 + 主要特征
第三段：详细数据描述
```

## 三、开头段写作

### 改写题目

**原题：**
> The bar chart shows the number of students studying abroad from China between 2010 and 2020.

**改写方法：**

#### 方法 1：同义替换
<ExampleSentence
  en="The bar chart illustrates the quantity of Chinese students pursuing overseas education from 2010 to 2020."
  cn="柱状图展示了2010年至2020年中国留学生的数量。"
  highlights={["illustrates", "quantity", "pursuing overseas education"]}
/>

#### 方法 2：改变句式
<ExampleSentence
  en="Between 2010 and 2020, the number of Chinese students studying abroad is depicted in the bar chart."
  cn="2010年至2020年间，中国留学生的数量在柱状图中呈现。"
  highlights={["is depicted in"]}
/>

### 概述（Overview）

**功能：** 总结图表的主要趋势或特征（2-3句话）

**模板句型：**

<ExampleSentence
  en="Overall, there was a significant increase in the number of students studying abroad during this period."
  cn="总体而言，这一时期留学生数量显著增加。"
  highlights={["Overall", "significant increase"]}
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="It is clear that China had the highest number of international students, while Japan ranked last."
  cn="很明显，中国的留学生数量最多，而日本排名最后。"
  highlights={["It is clear that", "ranked last"]}
/>

## 四、描述趋势

### 📈 上升趋势

| 动词 | 名词 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| increase | an increase | The number increased significantly. |
| rise | a rise | There was a sharp rise in sales. |
| grow | growth | The economy grew steadily. |
| climb | a climb | Prices climbed dramatically. |
| surge | a surge | A surge in demand was observed. |
| soar | - | Profits soared to record levels. |

**程度副词：**
- 大幅度：significantly, dramatically, sharply, considerably, substantially
- 中等：moderately, steadily
- 小幅度：slightly, marginally, gradually

<ExampleSentence
  en="The number of online shoppers increased dramatically from 100 million in 2015 to 500 million in 2020."
  cn="网购用户数量从2015年的1亿大幅增长到2020年的5亿。"
  highlights={["increased dramatically", "from...to..."]}
/>

### 📉 下降趋势

| 动词 | 名词 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| decrease | a decrease | Sales decreased by 20%. |
| decline | a decline | There was a decline in profits. |
| drop | a drop | Prices dropped sharply. |
| fall | a fall | A fall in demand occurred. |
| plunge | a plunge | Stock prices plunged. |
| dip | a dip | A slight dip was noted. |

<ExampleSentence
  en="The unemployment rate declined steadily from 8% in 2010 to 4% in 2020."
  cn="失业率从2010年的8%稳步下降到2020年的4%。"
  highlights={["declined steadily", "from...to..."]}

|------|------|------|

## 六、时间表达

### 具体时间点

- in 2020 / in January / in the 1990s
- on Monday / on January 1st
- at 3 PM / at the beginning of the year

### 时间段

- from 2010 to 2020 / between 2010 and 2020
- during the period / throughout the decade
- over the next five years
- for three months

### 时间顺序

- initially / at first / in the beginning
- then / after that / subsequently
- finally / eventually / by the end of the period

<ExampleSentence
  en="Initially, the figure stood at 100,000. It then rose gradually to 150,000 by 2015, before surging to 300,000 in 2020."
  cn="最初，这个数字为10万。然后逐渐上升到2015年的15万，之后在2020年激增至30万。"
  highlights={["Initially", "then", "before"]}
/>

## 七、范文示例

### 题目：柱状图 - 不同年龄段的互联网使用率

**图表描述：**
The bar chart shows the percentage of internet users in different age groups in 2020.
- 16-24: 98%
- 25-34: 95%
- 35-44: 88%
- 45-54: 75%
- 55-64: 60%
- 65+: 40%

---

**范文：**

The bar chart illustrates the proportion of internet users across various age groups in 2020. Overall, it is evident that internet usage decreases with age, with the youngest group showing the highest rate and the oldest group the lowest.

Looking at the details, young adults aged 16-24 had the highest internet usage rate at 98%, closely followed by the 25-34 age group at 95%. This indicates that nearly all individuals in these age brackets were active internet users. The 35-44 age group also demonstrated high usage, with 88% accessing the internet regularly.

In contrast, internet adoption was considerably lower among older age groups. The 45-54 age group showed a usage rate of 75%, while only 60% of those aged 55-64 used the internet. The most dramatic difference was observed in the 65+ category, where merely 40% were internet users, representing less than half the rate of the youngest group.

In conclusion, the data clearly reveals a strong correlation between age and internet usage, with younger generations being significantly more connected than their older counterparts. This digital divide highlights the need for initiatives to improve digital literacy among senior citizens.

## 八、常用句型模板

### 开头段

| 用途 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 改写题目 | The [图表类型] illustrates/shows/depicts... |
| 概述上升 | Overall, there was a significant increase in... |
| 概述下降 | In general, the figure declined considerably. |
| 概述对比 | It is clear that A was higher than B. |

### 主体段

| 用途 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 具体数据 | The figure stood at/reached/was... |
| 时间变化 | From 2010 to 2020, the number rose from...to... |
| 最高/最低 | X had the highest/lowest percentage, at... |
| 排名 | X ranked first, followed by Y and Z. |
| 比例 | X accounted for/made up/represented...% of the total. |

### 结尾段

| 用途 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 总结趋势 | In conclusion, the data reveals a clear trend of... |
| 总结对比 | To sum up, there were significant differences between... |
| 延伸思考 | This suggests that... / This highlights the need for... |

## 九、常见错误及改正

### ❌ 错误 1：主观评价

**错误：**
> "This is a good trend because more people are using the internet."

**改正：**
> "The data shows that internet usage increased significantly during this period."

**说明：** 图表描述应客观陈述事实，避免主观判断。

### ❌ 错误 2：遗漏概述

**错误：**
> 直接描述具体数据，没有总体概述

**改正：**
> 在开头或第二段加入概述句，总结主要趋势

### ❌ 错误 3：罗列所有数据

**错误：**
> 逐一列举每个数据点

**改正：**
> 选择关键数据（最高、最低、转折点），分组描述

### ❌ 错误 4：时态错误

**错误：**
> "The number increases from 2010 to 2020."（现在时）

**改正：**
> "The number increased from 2010 to 2020."（过去时）

**说明：** 描述过去的数据用过去时，描述图表本身用现在时。

## 十、练习题目

### 题目 1：折线图
**The line graph shows the average monthly temperature in Beijing throughout 2020.**

**要点：**
- 描述温度的季节性变化
- 指出最高和最低温度
- 说明整体趋势

### 题目 2：饼图
**The pie chart illustrates the distribution of household expenses in 2020.**
- Housing: 35%
- Food: 25%
- Transportation: 15%
- Entertainment: 10%
- Healthcare: 8%
- Others: 7%

**要点：**
- 指出最大和最小类别
- 比较各类别比例
- 总结支出分布特点

### 题目 3：表格
**The table shows the number of tourists visiting three cities from 2018 to 2020.**

| City | 2018 | 2019 | 2020 |
|------|------|------|------|
| Beijing | 5M | 6M | 3M |
| Shanghai | 4M | 5M | 2.5M |
| Guangzhou | 3M | 3.5M | 2M |

**要点：**
- 描述各城市游客数量变化
- 比较三个城市
- 分析2020年的特殊情况
