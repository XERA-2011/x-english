# 议论文写作 <DifficultyBadge level="B2" />

> 议论文是四级写作最常考的题型。掌握结构模板，拿分很稳。

## 万能结构（三段式）

```
第一段：引入话题 + 亮明观点
第二段：论据1 + 论据2（举例/数据）
第三段：总结观点 + 升华
```

---

## 每段怎么写

### 第一段：开头 + 观点

<ExampleSentence
  en="In recent years, online shopping has become increasingly popular among young people."
  cn="近年来，网上购物在年轻人中越来越流行。"
  highlights={["In recent years", "has become increasingly popular"]}
  note="现象描述法 — 最安全的开头方式"
/>

**观点句模板：**

| 模板 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| I firmly believe that... | I firmly believe that reading is more beneficial than watching videos. |
| From my perspective,... | From my perspective, part-time jobs help students develop practical skills. |

### 第二段：论据展开

<ExampleSentence
  en="First and foremost, online learning offers unparalleled flexibility."
  cn="首先，在线学习提供了无与伦比的灵活性。"
  highlights={["First and foremost"]}
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="For instance, my roommate successfully completed an online course while working part-time."
  cn="例如，我的室友在兼职的同时成功完成了一门在线课程。"
  highlights={["For instance"]}
  note="举例让论据更有说服力"
/>

### 第三段：总结

<ExampleSentence
  en="In conclusion, the benefits of online learning far outweigh its drawbacks."
  cn="总之，在线学习的好处远远超过其缺点。"
  highlights={["In conclusion", "far outweigh"]}
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="Only by embracing these changes can we thrive in the digital age."
  cn="只有拥抱这些变化，我们才能在数字时代蓬勃发展。"
  highlights={["Only by", "can we thrive"]}
  note="倒装句式升华 — 阅卷老师最爱"
/>

## 范文示例

**题目：Should universities replace traditional classrooms with online learning?**

:::info 📖 范文
In recent years, online learning has gained tremendous popularity, especially after the pandemic. While some people advocate for completely replacing traditional classrooms with online courses, I believe that a balanced approach combining both methods would be more beneficial.

**First and foremost, online learning offers unparalleled flexibility and convenience.** Students can access course materials anytime and anywhere. For instance, my friend Sarah, a working mother, successfully completed her master's degree through online courses while managing her career and family.

**Moreover, online education significantly reduces costs for both students and institutions.** According to a recent study, online courses cost approximately 30% less than traditional programs. This financial accessibility enables more people to pursue higher education.

**However, traditional classrooms offer irreplaceable benefits.** Face-to-face interaction fosters deeper understanding and immediate feedback. These social skills are crucial for personal development and future career success.

**In conclusion, universities should adopt a hybrid model that leverages the strengths of both approaches.** By combining these methods, we can create a more effective educational system that meets diverse student needs.
:::

## 更多常考话题范文

### 话题：社交媒体的影响

**Do the advantages of social media outweigh the disadvantages?**

:::info 📖 范文
In recent years, social media has become an integral part of our daily lives. From my perspective, while social media has its drawbacks, the advantages far outweigh the disadvantages.

**First and foremost, social media enables people to stay connected regardless of distance.** Students studying abroad can easily keep in touch with family and friends through platforms like WeChat. Furthermore, social media serves as an efficient tool for information sharing, allowing news to spread rapidly across the globe.

**However, we cannot ignore the potential risks of excessive social media use.** Privacy concerns and the spread of misinformation are growing problems. Some users may also become addicted, spending too much time scrolling instead of engaging in meaningful activities.

**In conclusion, social media is a double-edged sword.** The key lies in using it wisely and moderately. Only by developing good digital habits can we fully benefit from social media while minimizing its negative effects.
:::

---

### 话题：环境保护

**Who should be responsible for environmental protection: individuals or governments?**

:::info 📖 范文
Environmental protection has become one of the most pressing issues of our time. I firmly believe that both individuals and governments should share the responsibility for protecting our planet.

**On the one hand, governments play a crucial role in environmental protection.** They have the power to enact laws and regulations that limit pollution and promote sustainable development. For instance, China's ban on single-use plastics has significantly reduced plastic waste in major cities.

**On the other hand, individual actions are equally important.** Simple daily habits such as recycling, using public transportation, and reducing energy consumption can collectively make a tremendous difference. As the saying goes, every little bit helps.

**To sum up, environmental protection is a shared responsibility.** Governments should provide the framework through policies and infrastructure, while individuals should take action in their daily lives. Only by working together can we build a sustainable future for the next generation.
:::

## 实战练习
