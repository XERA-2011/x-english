# 应用文写作 Practical Writing <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

> 应用文是日常生活和工作中最常用的文体，包括书信、邮件、通知、申请等。

## 一、应用文类型

| 类型 | 用途 | 常见场景 |
|------|------|---------|
| 求职信 | Job Application Letter | 申请工作、实习 |
| 推荐信 | Recommendation Letter | 推荐人才、产品 |
| 投诉信 | Complaint Letter | 表达不满、要求解决 |
| 感谢信 | Thank-you Letter | 表达感激 |
| 邀请信 | Invitation Letter | 邀请参加活动 |
| 通知 | Notice/Announcement | 告知信息 |
| 申请信 | Application Letter | 申请奖学金、活动 |

---

## 二、书信格式

### 标准格式

```
发信人地址（可省略）
日期

收信人姓名
收信人地址（可省略）

称呼（Dear...）

正文第一段
正文第二段
正文第三段

结束语（Sincerely,）
签名
```

### 格式要点

**1. 日期格式**
- 美式：December 15, 2026
- 英式：15 December 2026

**2. 称呼**
- 知道姓名：Dear Mr. Smith, / Dear Professor Johnson,
- 不知道姓名：Dear Sir or Madam, / To Whom It May Concern,
- 非正式：Dear John, / Hi Sarah,

**3. 结束语**
- 正式：Sincerely, / Yours sincerely, / Respectfully,
- 半正式：Best regards, / Kind regards,
- 非正式：Best, / Cheers, / Warmly,

## 三、常见应用文写作

### 📧 类型 1：求职信 Job Application Letter

**结构：**
1. 说明写信目的（申请职位）
2. 介绍个人背景和优势
3. 表达期待和感谢

**范文：**

```
Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to apply for the Marketing Assistant position advertised on your company website. As a recent graduate with a degree in Marketing and relevant internship experience, I am confident that I would be a valuable addition to your team.

During my internship at ABC Company, I successfully managed social media campaigns that increased engagement by 40%. I also assisted in organizing promotional events and conducting market research. These experiences have equipped me with strong communication skills and the ability to work effectively in fast-paced environments.

I am particularly drawn to your company's innovative approach to digital marketing. I would welcome the opportunity to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to your team. Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to hearing from you.

Sincerely,
Li Ming
```

### 📧 类型 2：投诉信 Complaint Letter

**结构：**
1. 说明写信目的（投诉问题）
2. 详细描述问题
3. 提出解决方案或要求

**范文：**

```
Dear Customer Service Manager,

I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with a recent purchase I made from your online store. On November 20, I ordered a laptop (Order #12345), but the product I received was damaged and did not match the description on your website.

Upon opening the package, I noticed several scratches on the screen and a dent on the corner. Moreover, the laptop came with only 8GB of RAM instead of the advertised 16GB. I have attached photos of the damage for your reference.

I would appreciate it if you could either send me a replacement or provide a full refund. I have been a loyal customer for three years and hope this matter can be resolved promptly. Please contact me at your earliest convenience.

Sincerely,
Wang Fang
```

### 📧 类型 3：感谢信 Thank-you Letter

**结构：**
1. 表达感谢
2. 说明感谢的具体原因
3. 再次感谢并表达祝愿

**范文：**

```
Dear Professor Smith,

I am writing to express my sincere gratitude for your guidance and support during my research project. Your insightful feedback and encouragement were invaluable in helping me complete my thesis.

Your willingness to meet with me regularly, despite your busy schedule, demonstrated your dedication to student success. The research methods you taught me will undoubtedly benefit my future academic pursuits. I particularly appreciated your patience in explaining complex statistical concepts.

Once again, thank you for your mentorship. I hope to have the opportunity to work with you again in the future. I wish you all the best in your research and teaching.

Warmly,
Zhang Wei
```

### 📧 类型 4：邀请信 Invitation Letter

**结构：**
1. 发出邀请
2. 说明活动详情（时间、地点、内容）
3. 期待回复

**范文：**

```
Dear International Students,

We are delighted to invite you to our annual Cultural Festival, which will be held on Saturday, December 20, from 2:00 PM to 6:00 PM in the Student Center.

This year's festival will feature performances, food stalls, and exhibitions representing over 30 countries. It's a wonderful opportunity to celebrate diversity, share your culture, and make new friends. We especially encourage you to participate by showcasing traditional music, dance, or cuisine from your home country.

Please RSVP by December 10 by emailing us at culturalclub@university.edu. We look forward to seeing you there!

Best regards,
The Cultural Club Committee
```

## 四、通知写作 Notice/Announcement

### 格式

```
NOTICE / ANNOUNCEMENT

标题（居中）

正文（简洁明了）

发布单位
日期
```

### 范文：学生会活动通知

```
NOTICE

English Speech Contest

The Student Union is pleased to announce that an English Speech Contest will be held on Friday, January 15, 2027, at 3:00 PM in Lecture Hall A.

Topic: "The Role of Technology in Education"
Duration: 5-7 minutes per speaker
Prizes: First Prize (¥1,000), Second Prize (¥500), Third Prize (¥300)

Interested students should register by emailing studentunion@university.edu before January 5. Please include your name, major, and contact information.

We look forward to your participation!

The Student Union
December 20, 2026
```

**关键要素：**
- 标题清晰
- 时间、地点明确
- 活动内容简洁
- 报名方式清楚
- 落款和日期

## 五、电子邮件写作 Email Writing

### 邮件结构

```
Subject: 简洁明了的主题

Dear [Name],

开头问候（可选）
正文第一段：说明写信目的
正文第二段：详细信息
结尾：表达期待或感谢

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### 范文：请假邮件

```
Subject: Request for Leave - Li Ming

Dear Professor Johnson,

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to request a leave of absence from your Marketing class on December 18 due to a family emergency.

My grandmother has been hospitalized, and I need to travel to my hometown to be with my family. I understand that we have a group presentation scheduled for that day, and I have already coordinated with my team members to present my part in advance.

I will catch up on any missed material and submit all assignments on time. Thank you for your understanding. Please let me know if you need any additional information.

Best regards,
Li Ming
Student ID: 2024001
```

**邮件写作技巧：**
1. **主题行要具体**：避免"Hello"或"Question"
2. **开头礼貌**：简短问候或直接说明目的
3. **正文简洁**：一个段落一个要点
4. **结尾明确**：说明期待的回复或行动
5. **检查拼写**：特别是收件人姓名

## 六、常用句型汇总

### 开头句型

| 用途 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 申请 | I am writing to apply for... |
| 询问 | I am writing to inquire about... |
| 投诉 | I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with... |
| 感谢 | I am writing to express my sincere gratitude for... |
| 邀请 | We are pleased to invite you to... |
| 建议 | I am writing to offer some suggestions regarding... |

### 正文句型

| 用途 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 说明原因 | The reason why... is that... |
| 提供信息 | I would like to inform you that... |
| 表达能力 | I am confident that I have the skills to... |
| 提出要求 | I would appreciate it if you could... |
| 说明影响 | This has caused considerable inconvenience. |

### 结尾句型

| 用途 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 期待回复 | I look forward to hearing from you. |
| 表达感谢 | Thank you for your time and consideration. |
| 表达歉意 | I apologize for any inconvenience caused. |
| 提供联系 | Please feel free to contact me if you need further information. |
| 表达祝愿 | I wish you all the best. |

## 七、写作检查清单

### ✅ 格式检查
- [ ] 日期和称呼格式正确
- [ ] 段落分明，每段一个要点
- [ ] 结束语和签名完整
- [ ] 邮件主题行清晰（如适用）

### ✅ 内容检查
- [ ] 写信目的明确
- [ ] 信息完整（时间、地点、联系方式等）
- [ ] 语气得体（正式/半正式/非正式）
- [ ] 逻辑清晰，易于理解

### ✅ 语言检查
- [ ] 没有明显语法错误
- [ ] 拼写正确（特别是人名、地名）
- [ ] 使用恰当的礼貌用语
- [ ] 避免口语化表达（在正式信件中）
