# 被动语态 Passive Voice <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

> 被动语态用来强调**动作的承受者**而非执行者，或者在不知道/不需要说明执行者时使用。

## 一、构成规则

**结构：be + 过去分词 (p.p.)**

| 时态 | 主动语态 | 被动语态 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 一般现在时 | He **cleans** the room. | The room **is cleaned**. |
| 一般过去时 | He **cleaned** the room. | The room **was cleaned**. |
| 一般将来时 | He **will clean** the room. | The room **will be cleaned**. |
| 现在进行时 | He **is cleaning** the room. | The room **is being cleaned**. |
| 现在完成时 | He **has cleaned** the room. | The room **has been cleaned**. |
| 情态动词 | He **can clean** the room. | The room **can be cleaned**. |

## 三、主动变被动的步骤

```
主动：Tom    wrote    this letter.
      主语    谓语      宾语
        ↓       ↓        ↓
被动：This letter  was written  (by Tom).
      新主语       被动谓语     施动者（可省略）
```

:::info 三步变换法
1. 将原句的**宾语**变为新句的**主语**
2. 谓语变为 **be + p.p.**（be 的时态与原句保持一致）
3. 原来的主语变为 **by + 施动者**（可省略）
:::

## 四、用法详解

### 1. 强调动作承受者

<ExampleSentence
  en="The window was broken by the children."
  cn="窗户被孩子们打破了。"
  highlights={["was broken"]}
  note="重点在于'窗户被打破'，而非谁打破的"
/>

### 2. 不知道或不需要说明执行者

<ExampleSentence
  en="My bike was stolen last night."
  cn="我的自行车昨晚被偷了。"
  highlights={["was stolen"]}
  note="不知道是谁偷的，省略 by..."
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="English is spoken all over the world."
  cn="英语在全世界被使用。"
  highlights={["is spoken"]}
  note="执行者是'全世界的人'，无需特别指明"
/>

### 3. 各时态的被动语态

<ExampleSentence
  en="The report will be finished by Friday."
  cn="报告将在周五之前完成。"
  highlights={["will be finished"]}
  note="一般将来时的被动语态：will be + p.p."
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="The road is being repaired right now."
  cn="这条路目前正在修缮中。"
  highlights={["is being repaired"]}
  note="现在进行时的被动语态：is being + p.p."
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="The homework has been submitted."
  cn="作业已经提交了。"
  highlights={["has been submitted"]}
  note="现在完成时的被动语态：has been + p.p."
/>

### 4. 含情态动词的被动语态

<ExampleSentence

## 五、常见被动语态固定搭配

| 搭配 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| be made of | 由…制成（看得出原料） | The desk **is made of** wood. |
| be made from | 由…制成（看不出原料） | Paper **is made from** wood. |
| be known as | 被称为 | He **is known as** the "King of Pop." |
| be covered with | 被…覆盖 | The ground **is covered with** snow. |
| be interested in | 对…感兴趣 | She **is interested in** music. |
| be surprised at | 对…感到惊讶 | We **were surprised at** the news. |

<Quiz

## 七、小结

:::tip 记忆口诀
被动语态 be + done；
强调承受不说人；
各种时态 be 变化；
情态动词加 be done；
by 后施动可省略。
:::
