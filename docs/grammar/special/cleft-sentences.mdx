# 强调句 Cleft Sentences <DifficultyBadge level="B2" />

> 强调句用 **It is/was ... that/who ...** 结构来突出句子中的某个成分。

## 一、基本结构

**It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其余部分**

原句：**Tom** met **Mary** **in the park** **yesterday**.

| 强调内容 | 强调句 |
|---------|-------|
| 强调主语 | **It was Tom** who/that met Mary in the park yesterday. |
| 强调宾语 | **It was Mary** who/that Tom met in the park yesterday. |
| 强调地点 | **It was in the park** that Tom met Mary yesterday. |
| 强调时间 | **It was yesterday** that Tom met Mary in the park. |

:::info 核心规则
- 强调**人**时，可用 **who** 或 **that**
- 强调**物/时间/地点**时，只能用 **that**
- **不能强调谓语动词**
:::

## 二、用法详解

### 强调主语

<ExampleSentence
  en="It was my mother who/that taught me how to cook."
  cn="是我妈妈教我如何做饭的。"
  highlights={["It was", "my mother", "who", "that"]}
  note="强调是'妈妈'而不是别人"
/>

### 强调宾语

<ExampleSentence
  en="It was this book that I bought yesterday."
  cn="我昨天买的就是这本书。"
  highlights={["It was", "this book", "that"]}
  note="强调买的是'这本书'而非其他"
/>

### 强调时间状语

<ExampleSentence
  en="It was in 2020 that she graduated from university."
  cn="她是在2020年大学毕业的。"
  highlights={["It was", "in 2020", "that"]}
  note="强调毕业的时间是'2020年'"
/>

### 强调原因状语

<ExampleSentence
  en="It was because of the heavy rain that the game was canceled."
  cn="正是因为大雨，比赛才被取消了。"
  highlights={["It was", "because of the heavy rain", "that"]}
  note="强调取消的原因是'大雨'"
/>

## 三、如何判断是否为强调句

**方法：去掉 It is/was ... that，看剩下的部分是否构成完整句子。**

- ✅ It was Tom **that** met Mary yesterday. → 去掉后：Tom met Mary yesterday. ✓（完整句，是强调句）
- ❌ It is obvious **that** she is lying. → 去掉后：obvious she is lying. ✗（不完整，是主语从句）

## 四、强调句的疑问形式

**Is/Was it + 被强调部分 + that ...?**

<ExampleSentence
  en="Was it yesterday that you met her?"
  cn="你是昨天遇到她的吗？"
  highlights={["Was it", "yesterday", "that"]}
/>

**特殊疑问句：疑问词 + is/was it that ...?**

<ExampleSentence
  en="Who was it that broke the window?"
  cn="打破窗户的到底是谁？"
  highlights={["Who was it that"]}
  note="对主语提问的强调句特殊疑问句"
/>
