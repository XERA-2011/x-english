# 过去进行时 Past Continuous <DifficultyBadge level="A2" />

> 过去进行时用来表达**过去某一时刻正在进行的动作**，或**为另一个过去事件提供背景**。

## 一、构成规则

**结构：was / were + 动词-ing**

| 人称 | 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| I / He / She / It | I **was reading** | I **wasn't reading** | **Was** I reading? |
| You / We / They | They **were reading** | They **weren't reading** | **Were** they reading? |

## 三、用法详解

### 1. 过去某一时刻正在进行的动作

<ExampleSentence
  en="At 8 PM last night, I was watching a movie."
  cn="昨晚八点我正在看电影。"
  highlights={["was watching", "At 8 PM last night"]}
  note="at + 过去具体时间 = 过去进行时的经典标志"
/>

### 2. when / while 引导的时间状语从句（最核心用法）

**长动作（进行时）+ 短动作（一般过去时）**

<ExampleSentence
  en="I was taking a shower when someone knocked on the door."
  cn="我正在洗澡的时候，有人敲门了。"
  highlights={["was taking", "knocked"]}
  note="was taking（长动作，进行时）；knocked（短动作，一般过去时）"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="While I was walking home, it started to rain."
  cn="我走路回家的时候，开始下雨了。"
  highlights={["was walking", "started"]}
  note="while 引导进行时从句，主句用一般过去时"
/>

**两个长动作同时进行**

<ExampleSentence
  en="While she was cooking, he was cleaning the house."
  cn="她做饭的时候，他在打扫房子。"
  highlights={["was cooking", "was cleaning"]}
  note="两个同时进行的动作都用过去进行时"
/>

### 3. 描绘故事背景

<ExampleSentence
  en="It was snowing heavily. The wind was blowing. Nobody was walking on the street."
  cn="大雪纷飞。风在呼啸。街上没有人。"
  highlights={["was snowing", "was blowing", "was walking"]}
  note="用过去进行时铺设故事场景，营造画面感"
/>

## 四、when vs while

| 连词 | 搭配 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **when** | 通常接**短动作**（一般过去时） | I was sleeping **when** the alarm rang. |
| **while** | 通常接**长动作**（过去进行时） | **While** I was sleeping, the alarm rang. |

:::info 简记方式
- **when** + 一般过去时（短动作打断长动作）
- **while** + 过去进行时（长动作作为背景）
:::

## 五、常见时间标志词

| 标志词 | 示例 |
|--------|------|
| at + 过去时间点 | I was studying **at 10 PM**. |
| when + 过去时从句 | She was reading **when** I arrived. |
| while + 进行时从句 | **While** we were talking, he left. |
| at that time / at that moment | **At that moment**, he was driving. |

/>

## 七、小结

:::tip 记忆口诀
过去进行 was/were -ing；
过去某刻正发生；
when 接短 while 接长；
故事背景用它棒。
:::
