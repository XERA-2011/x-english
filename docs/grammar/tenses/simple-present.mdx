# 一般现在时 Simple Present <DifficultyBadge level="A1" />

> 一般现在时是英语中最基础、最常用的时态，用来表达**经常性的动作、客观事实和普遍真理**。

## 一、构成规则

| 人称 | 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| I / You / We / They | I **work** | I **don't work** | **Do** you work? |
| He / She / It | She **works** | She **doesn't work** | **Does** she work? |

:::info 第三人称单数规则
- 一般加 **-s**：work → work**s**
- 以 s/sh/ch/x/o 结尾加 **-es**：go → go**es**，watch → watch**es**
- 以辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i 加 **-es**：study → stud**ies**
:::

## 三、用法详解

### 1. 经常性、习惯性动作

常与频率副词搭配：**always, usually, often, sometimes, seldom, never**

<ExampleSentence
  en="I usually get up at 7 o'clock."
  cn="我通常七点起床。"
  highlights={["usually", "get up"]}
  note="频率副词 usually 放在实义动词 get up 之前"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="He never drinks coffee."
  cn="他从不喝咖啡。"
  highlights={["never", "drinks"]}
  note="第三人称单数：drink → drinks"
/>

### 2. 客观事实和普遍真理

<ExampleSentence
  en="The earth goes around the sun."
  cn="地球围绕太阳转。"
  highlights={["goes"]}
  note="客观事实，永远用一般现在时"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius."
  cn="水在100摄氏度时沸腾。"
  highlights={["boils"]}
/>

### 3. 时刻表、日程安排

<ExampleSentence
  en="The train leaves at 9:15 tomorrow morning."
  cn="火车明天早上9:15出发。"
  highlights={["leaves"]}
  note="按时刻表运行的交通工具，用一般现在时表将来"
/>

## 四、常见时间标志词

| 标志词 | 示例 |
|--------|------|
| every day / week / month | I exercise **every day**. |
| always / usually / often | She **always** arrives on time. |
| sometimes / seldom / never | They **never** eat fast food. |
| on weekends / on Mondays | We play football **on weekends**. |

## 六、小结

:::tip 记忆口诀
一般现在时，经常习惯要牢记；
三单动词加 s，否定疑问用 do/does；
客观真理不会变，时刻表里看将来。
:::
