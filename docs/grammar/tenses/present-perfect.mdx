# 现在完成时 Present Perfect <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

> 现在完成时用来表达**过去发生但与现在有联系的动作**，强调的是动作对现在的影响或结果。

## 一、构成规则

**结构：have / has + 过去分词 (p.p.)**

| 人称 | 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| I / You / We / They | I **have finished** | I **haven't finished** | **Have** you finished? |
| He / She / It | She **has finished** | She **hasn't finished** | **Has** she finished? |

### 过去分词变化规则

| 情况 | 规则 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| 规则动词 | 与过去式相同（加 -ed） | worked, studied, stopped |
| 不规则动词 | 需要逐个记忆 | go → gone, see → seen, write → written |

### 常见不规则过去分词

| 原形 | 过去式 | 过去分词 | 原形 | 过去式 | 过去分词 |
|------|--------|---------|------|--------|---------|
| be | was/were | **been** | do | did | **done** |
| go | went | **gone** | see | saw | **seen** |
| take | took | **taken** | write | wrote | **written** |
| eat | ate | **eaten** | give | gave | **given** |
| speak | spoke | **spoken** | break | broke | **broken** |

## 三、用法详解

### 1. 过去的动作对现在造成的影响

<ExampleSentence
  en="I have lost my key. (So I can't open the door now.)"
  cn="我把钥匙弄丢了。（所以现在我打不开门。）"
  highlights={["have lost"]}
  note="强调丢钥匙这件事对现在的影响——门打不开"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="She has gone to London. (She is not here now.)"
  cn="她去伦敦了。（她现在不在这里。）"
  highlights={["has gone"]}
  note="has gone to = 去了某地（人不在此处）"
/>

### 2. 从过去持续到现在的状态（搭配 for / since）

<ExampleSentence
  en="I have lived in this city for five years."
  cn="我在这个城市住了五年了。"
  highlights={["have lived", "for five years"]}
  note="for + 一段时间，表示持续了多久"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="She has worked here since 2019."
  cn="她从2019年起就在这里工作了。"
  highlights={["has worked", "since 2019"]}
  note="since + 过去时间点，表示从那时起一直持续"
/>

### 3. 经历（搭配 ever / never / before）

<ExampleSentence
  en="Have you ever been to Japan?"
  cn="你去过日本吗？"
  highlights={["Have", "ever been"]}
  note="have been to = 曾经去过（人已回来）"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="I have never seen such a beautiful sunset."
  cn="我从未见过如此美丽的日落。"
  highlights={["have never seen"]}
/>

### 4. 到目前为止的重复动作（搭配 already / yet / just）

<ExampleSentence
  en="I have already finished my homework."
  cn="我已经完成作业了。"
  highlights={["have already finished"]}
  note="already 用于肯定句，表示'已经'"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="Have you eaten breakfast yet?"
  cn="你吃早饭了吗？"
  highlights={["Have", "yet"]}
  note="yet 用于疑问句和否定句，表示'还、已经'"
/>

## 四、have been to vs have gone to

| 表达 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| have been to | 曾经去过（已回来） | I **have been to** Paris twice. |
| have gone to | 去了某地（人不在） | She **has gone to** Paris. (She's there now.) |

## 五、现在完成时 vs 一般过去时

:::warning 重要区别
| 现在完成时 | 一般过去时 |
|-----------|----------|
| 强调对**现在**的影响 | 强调**过去**的事实 |
| 不能与具体过去时间连用 | 可以与具体过去时间连用 |
| I **have lost** my key. (现在找不到) | I **lost** my key yesterday. (只说过去的事) |
:::

## 六、常见时间标志词

| 标志词 | 示例 |
|--------|------|
| already | I've **already** done it. |
| yet | Have you finished **yet**? |
| just | She has **just** arrived. |
| ever / never | Have you **ever** tried sushi? |
| for + 时间段 | I've known him **for** years. |
| since + 时间点 | She's lived here **since** 2020. |
| so far / up to now | **So far**, everything is fine. |

## 七、实战练习

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="She _____ in this company since 2018."
  options={["works", "worked", "has worked", "is working"]}
  answer="has worked"
  explanation="since 2018 是现在完成时的标志词，表示从2018年持续到现在。主语 She 用 has worked。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="— Where is Tom? — He _____ to the library."
  options={["has been", "has gone", "went", "goes"]}
  answer="has gone"
  explanation="has gone to = 去了某地（人不在这里）。has been to = 曾经去过（已回来）。Tom 不在，所以用 has gone to。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="I _____ never _____ (see) such a tall building before."
  answer="have, seen"
  explanation="have never seen 表示'从未见过'，是现在完成时表经历的典型用法。see → seen（不规则变化）。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="They _____ (live) in Shanghai for ten years."
  answer="have lived"
  explanation="for ten years 表示持续时间，搭配现在完成时 have lived，表示从过去一直住到现在。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="true-false"
  question="「I have bought this book yesterday.」这句话语法正确。"
  options={["True", "False"]}
  answer="False"
  explanation="现在完成时不能与具体的过去时间（yesterday）连用。应改为：I bought this book yesterday.（一般过去时）或 I have already bought this book.（现在完成时，去掉 yesterday）。"
/>

## 八、小结

:::tip 记忆口诀
现在完成 have/has done；
for 接时段 since 接点；
already 肯定 yet 否问；
gone 去没回 been 去过了；
过去影响到如今，不加具体过去时。
:::
