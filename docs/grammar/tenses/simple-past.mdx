# 一般过去时 Simple Past <DifficultyBadge level="A1" />

> 一般过去时用来表达**过去某个时间发生的动作或存在的状态**，是英语中最常用的时态之一。

## 一、构成规则

### 规则动词：词尾加 -ed

| 情况 | 规则 | 例子 |
|------|------|------|
| 一般情况 | 加 **-ed** | work → work**ed** |
| 以 e 结尾 | 加 **-d** | live → live**d** |
| 辅音 + y 结尾 | 变 y 为 i 加 **-ed** | study → stud**ied** |
| 重读闭音节结尾 | 双写末尾辅音加 **-ed** | stop → stop**ped** |

### 不规则动词（需要逐个记忆）

| 原形 | 过去式 | 原形 | 过去式 |
|------|--------|------|--------|
| go | **went** | come | **came** |
| have | **had** | do | **did** |
| see | **saw** | get | **got** |
| make | **made** | take | **took** |
| know | **knew** | think | **thought** |
| say | **said** | give | **gave** |

### 句型结构

| 人称 | 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| 所有人称 | I **worked** | I **didn't work** | **Did** you work? |
| | She **went** | She **didn't go** | **Did** she go? |

:::info 核心要点
- 否定句和疑问句中，动词**恢复原形**（did 已经承担了过去时标记）
- ❌ Did you went? → ✅ Did you **go**?
- ❌ She didn't went. → ✅ She didn't **go**.
:::

## 三、用法详解

### 1. 过去某个时间发生的动作

常与**明确的过去时间标志词**搭配。

<ExampleSentence
  en="I bought a new phone yesterday."
  cn="我昨天买了一部新手机。"
  highlights={["bought", "yesterday"]}
  note="buy 的过去式是 bought（不规则变化）"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="They moved to Beijing in 2020."
  cn="他们在2020年搬到了北京。"
  highlights={["moved", "in 2020"]}
  note="move 以 e 结尾，直接加 -d"
/>

### 2. 过去的习惯或经常性动作

<ExampleSentence
  en="When I was young, I often played football after school."
  cn="小时候，我经常放学后踢足球。"
  highlights={["was", "played"]}
  note="when I was young 表示过去的时间段，全句使用过去时"
/>

### 3. 过去连续发生的动作

<ExampleSentence
  en="He got up, brushed his teeth, and left for work."
  cn="他起床，刷了牙，然后去上班了。"
  highlights={["got up", "brushed", "left"]}
  note="连续发生的动作都使用一般过去时"
/>

### 4. 与 ago 搭配

<ExampleSentence
  en="She graduated from college two years ago."
  cn="她两年前大学毕业了。"
  highlights={["graduated", "ago"]}
  note="ago 表示'之前'，永远与一般过去时搭配"
/>

## 四、常见时间标志词

| 标志词 | 示例 |
|--------|------|
| yesterday | I saw him **yesterday**. |
| last week / month / year | We traveled **last summer**. |
| ... ago | He called me **two hours ago**. |
| in + 过去年份 | She was born **in 1998**. |
| just now | I met her **just now**. |
| the other day | I ran into Tom **the other day**. |

## 五、be 动词的过去式

| 人称 | 过去式 | 否定 |
|------|--------|------|
| I / He / She / It | **was** | **wasn't** |
| You / We / They | **were** | **weren't** |

<ExampleSentence
  en="I was very tired last night."
  cn="我昨晚非常累。"
  highlights={["was"]}
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="They were not at home when I called."
  cn="我打电话时他们不在家。"
  highlights={["were not"]}
/>

## 七、小结

:::tip 记忆口诀
一般过去时，过去发生要牢记；
规则动词加 -ed，不规则的靠积累；
否定疑问 did 来帮，动词还原用原形；
ago / last / yesterday，看到标志就过去。
:::
