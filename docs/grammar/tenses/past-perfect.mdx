# 过去完成时 Past Perfect <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

> 过去完成时用来表达**在过去某个时间点之前已经完成的动作**，即"过去的过去"。

## 一、构成规则

**结构：had + 过去分词 (p.p.)**

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | 主语 + **had** + p.p. | I **had finished** the work. |
| 否定句 | 主语 + **hadn't** + p.p. | She **hadn't eaten** yet. |
| 疑问句 | **Had** + 主语 + p.p.? | **Had** you seen the movie? |

:::info 关键概念
过去完成时描述的是**两个过去事件中更早发生的那个**。时间线上：过去完成时 → 一般过去时 → 现在。
:::

## 三、用法详解

### 1. 在过去某事之前已经完成的动作

<ExampleSentence
  en="When I got to the station, the train had already left."
  cn="当我到达车站时，火车已经开走了。"
  highlights={["had already left", "got"]}
  note="got（到站）是过去的事；had left（火车开走）发生得更早 → 过去的过去"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="She had never seen the ocean before she moved to the coast."
  cn="搬到海边之前，她从未见过大海。"
  highlights={["had never seen", "moved"]}
  note="moved（搬家）是过去的事；had never seen（没见过海）发生在搬家之前"
/>

### 2. 与 by the time 搭配

<ExampleSentence
  en="By the time we arrived, the movie had started."
  cn="等我们到的时候，电影已经开始了。"
  highlights={["had started", "By the time"]}
  note="by the time 引导一般过去时，主句用过去完成时"
/>

### 3. 与 after / before 搭配

<ExampleSentence
  en="After he had finished dinner, he went for a walk."
  cn="吃完晚饭后，他去散步了。"
  highlights={["had finished", "went"]}
  note="after 引导的从句（先发生的）用过去完成时"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="I had studied English for six years before I went to college."
  cn="上大学之前，我已经学了六年英语了。"
  highlights={["had studied", "before"]}
  note="before 引导的从句（后发生的）用一般过去时"
/>

### 4. 表示过去未实现的愿望

<ExampleSentence
  en="I had hoped to visit Paris, but I couldn't afford it."
  cn="我本来希望去巴黎的，但负担不起。"
  highlights={["had hoped"]}
  note="had hoped 表示过去的愿望，但最终未实现"
/>

## 四、时间线对比

```
过去完成时         一般过去时         现在
  (更早)            (较近)
    ↓                 ↓               ↓
had left          I arrived        (now)
─────────────────────────────────────→
```

## 五、常见时间标志词

| 标志词 | 示例 |
|--------|------|
| by the time | **By the time** I arrived, she had left. |
| before | He had eaten **before** I came. |
| after | **After** she had finished, she left. |
| already | They had **already** gone home. |
| never ... before | I had **never** been there **before**. |
| by + 过去时间 | He had learned 1000 words **by** age 10. |

## 六、实战练习

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="By the time we got there, the concert _____."
  options={["already started", "has already started", "had already started", "was starting"]}
  answer="had already started"
  explanation="by the time + 一般过去时（got），主句用过去完成时（had started），表示更早发生的事。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="She told me that she _____ the book."
  options={["reads", "read", "has read", "had read"]}
  answer="had read"
  explanation="told 是一般过去时，她读书这件事发生在告诉我之前（过去的过去），用过去完成时 had read。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="After he _____ (finish) his homework, he played video games."
  answer="had finished"
  explanation="after 引导先发生的动作，用过去完成时 had finished；主句是后发生的，用一般过去时 played。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="I _____ never _____ (fly) in a plane before last summer."
  answer="had, flown"
  explanation="before last summer 指过去之前的经历，用过去完成时。fly → flew → flown。"
/>

## 七、小结

:::tip 记忆口诀
过去完成 had + done；
过去之前就发生；
by the time / before / after；
先发生的 had 来标。
:::
