# 名词性从句 Noun Clauses <DifficultyBadge level="B2" />

> 名词性从句在句中充当名词的角色，可作主语、宾语、表语或同位语。

## 一、引导词分类

| 引导词 | 功能 | 示例 |
|--------|------|------|
| **that** | 无含义，只起连接作用 | I know **that** he is honest. |
| **whether/if** | 是否 | I wonder **whether** she will come. |
| **what** | 什么 / …的东西 | **What** he said surprised me. |
| **who/whom** | 谁 | I don't know **who** did it. |
| **when/where/how/why** | 何时/何地/如何/为何 | Tell me **where** you live. |

## 二、宾语从句（最常考）

放在动词后面，充当宾语。

<ExampleSentence
  en="I believe that he is telling the truth."
  cn="我相信他说的是真话。"
  highlights={["that he is telling the truth"]}
  note="that 从句作 believe 的宾语，that 可省略"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="She asked me whether I had finished the report."
  cn="她问我是否完成了报告。"
  highlights={["whether I had finished"]}
  note="宾语从句表示'是否'，用 whether/if 引导"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="Can you tell me where the nearest hospital is?"
  cn="你能告诉我最近的医院在哪吗？"
  highlights={["where the nearest hospital is"]}
  note="宾语从句用陈述语序（不倒装）：where + 主语 + 谓语"
/>

:::warning 宾语从句两大考点
1. **陈述语序**：从句中主语在谓语前面。 ❌ where is he → ✅ where he is
2. **时态一致**：主句过去时，从句一般也用过去时（客观真理除外）
:::

## 三、主语从句

放在句首，充当主语。

<ExampleSentence
  en="What he said made everyone laugh."
  cn="他说的话让大家都笑了。"
  highlights={["What he said"]}
  note="what 从句整体作主语"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="It is obvious that she is lying."
  cn="很明显她在说谎。"
  highlights={["It", "that she is lying"]}
  note="It 作形式主语，真正主语是 that 从句（更常用的写法）"
/>

## 四、表语从句

放在 be 动词后面，充当表语。

<ExampleSentence
  en="The problem is that we don't have enough money."
  cn="问题是我们没有足够的钱。"
  highlights={["that we don't have enough money"]}
  note="that 从句作 is 的表语"
/>

## 五、同位语从句

解释说明前面的抽象名词（news, fact, idea, hope, belief 等）。

<ExampleSentence
  en="The news that he had passed the exam made us happy."
  cn="他通过考试的消息让我们很高兴。"
  highlights={["that he had passed the exam"]}
  note="that 从句解释 news 的内容。注意与定语从句的区别"
/>

:::info 同位语从句 vs 定语从句
- **同位语从句**：that 不作任何成分，解释名词的内容（that = 即）
- **定语从句**：that 在从句中充当主语或宾语（that = 代替先行词）

对比：
- The news **that** he told me was exciting. → 定语从句（that = news，作 told 的宾语）
- The news **that** he won the prize was exciting. → 同位语从句（解释 news 的内容）
:::

## 六、实战练习

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="Could you tell me _____ the meeting will start?"
  options={["that when", "when", "when will", "will when"]}
  answer="when"
  explanation="宾语从句用 when 引导，且必须用陈述语序：when the meeting will start。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="_____ she said at the meeting surprised everyone."
  options={["That", "What", "Which", "How"]}
  answer="What"
  explanation="what 引导主语从句，what = the thing that。what 在从句中作 said 的宾语。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="The fact _____ the earth goes around the sun is known to all."
  options={["which", "what", "that", "how"]}
  answer="that"
  explanation="这是同位语从句，解释 fact 的具体内容。that 不作任何成分，仅起连接作用。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="I don't know _____ he will come or not. (是否)"
  answer="whether"
  explanation="'是否'用 whether 引导。注意：whether...or not 是固定搭配，此处不能用 if。"
/>
