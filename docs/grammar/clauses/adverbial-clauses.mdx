# 状语从句 Adverbial Clauses <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

> 状语从句在句中充当状语，修饰主句的动作或状态，按功能分为时间、条件、原因、让步等。

## 一、时间状语从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| when | 当…时 | **When** I arrived, he had left. |
| while | 在…期间 | **While** she was reading, I was cooking. |
| before | 在…之前 | Finish it **before** you go. |
| after | 在…之后 | **After** he graduated, he got a job. |
| as soon as | 一…就… | I'll call you **as soon as** I arrive. |
| until / not...until | 直到…/ 直到…才 | Wait here **until** I come back. |

<ExampleSentence
  en="I didn't go to bed until I finished my homework."
  cn="我做完作业才睡觉。"
  highlights={["didn't", "until", "finished"]}
  note="not...until = 直到…才。主句否定，从句肯定"
/>

## 二、条件状语从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| if | 如果 | **If** it rains, we'll stay home. |
| unless | 除非（= if...not） | I won't go **unless** you come with me. |
| as long as | 只要 | You can go **as long as** you finish your work. |
| provided (that) | 假如；条件是 | I'll help you **provided that** you ask politely. |

<ExampleSentence
  en="If you study hard, you will pass the exam."
  cn="如果你努力学习，你会通过考试的。"
  highlights={["If", "will pass"]}
  note="条件状语从句用一般现在时表将来（主将从现）"
/>

:::warning 主将从现
条件和时间状语从句中，**从句不能用 will**，要用一般现在时代替将来时。
- ❌ If it **will rain** tomorrow...
- ✅ If it **rains** tomorrow...
:::

## 三、原因状语从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 语气强弱 |
|------|------|---------|
| because | 因为 | 最强（回答 why） |
| since | 既然 | 较弱（已知原因） |
| as | 由于 | 最弱（顺便提及） |

<ExampleSentence
  en="Since everyone is here, let's begin the meeting."
  cn="既然大家都到了，我们开始开会吧。"
  highlights={["Since"]}
  note="since 引导已知原因，语气较弱"
/>

## 四、让步状语从句

| 连词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| although / though | 虽然…但是 | **Although** he is young, he is very capable. |
| even though / even if | 即使 | **Even if** it rains, I will go. |
| no matter what/how | 无论… | **No matter how** hard it is, don't give up. |

<ExampleSentence
  en="Although she was tired, she continued working."
  cn="虽然她很累，但她继续工作。"
  highlights={["Although"]}
  note="although 和 but 不能同时使用！用了 although 就不能再加 but"
/>

:::warning 常见错误
❌ **Although** he is rich, **but** he is not happy.
✅ **Although** he is rich, he is not happy.
✅ He is rich, **but** he is not happy.
:::

## 五、目的状语从句

| 连词 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| so that | 以便；为了 |
| in order that | 为了 |

<ExampleSentence
  en="She got up early so that she could catch the first bus."
  cn="她早起是为了赶上第一班公交车。"
  highlights={["so that", "could catch"]}
  note="so that 引导目的从句，从句中常用 can/could/will/would"
/>

## 六、结果状语从句

| 连词 | 含义 |
|------|------|
| so...that | 如此…以至于 |
| such...that | 如此…以至于 |

<ExampleSentence
  en="He was so tired that he fell asleep immediately."
  cn="他太累了以至于立刻就睡着了。"
  highlights={["so tired", "that"]}
  note="so + 形容词/副词 + that"
/>

<ExampleSentence
  en="It was such a good movie that I watched it twice."
  cn="这部电影太好看了，我看了两遍。"
  highlights={["such a good movie", "that"]}
  note="such + a/an + 形容词 + 名词 + that"
/>
