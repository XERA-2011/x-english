## 1. 校园生活 <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

<AudioPlayer 
  src="/audio/listening/dictation-01.mp3"
  transcript={`

## 2. 健康生活 <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

<AudioPlayer 
  src="/audio/listening/dictation-02.mp3"
  transcript={`

<AudioPlayer
