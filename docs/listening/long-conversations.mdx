<AudioPlayer

## 二、高频表达

### 表示同意 / 反对
- I couldn't agree more. (完全同意) / I see it differently. (看法不同)
- That makes sense. (有道理) / That's not necessarily true. (不一定)

### 表示建议 / 犹豫
- Why don't you...? (为什么不...) / I'm not sure... (我不确定...)
- It might be a good idea to... (也许...是个好主意) / Let me think about it. (让我想想)
