# 短对话理解 Short Conversations <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

## 一、核心场景与词汇

### 1. 校园生活 Campus Life

**高频词汇：**
- assignment（作业）、deadline（截止日期）、extension（延期）
- library（图书馆）、dormitory（宿舍）、cafeteria（食堂）
- professor（教授）、lecture（讲座）、seminar（研讨会）

<AudioPlayer 
  src="/audio/listening/conversation-01.mp3"
  transcript={`
**M:** Excuse me, I'd like to renew these books.
**W:** I'm sorry, but one of them has been reserved by another student. You'll have to return it today.
**Q:** What does the woman mean?
  `}
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="What does the woman mean?"
  options={[
    "The man can renew all the books",
    "The man must return one book today",
    "The books are not available",
    "The man needs to pay a fine"
  ]}
  answer="The man must return one book today"
  explanation="关键词：reserved（被预约）, return it today（今天归还）。"
/>

<AudioPlayer 
  src="/audio/listening/conversation-02.mp3"
  transcript={`
**W:** Professor Smith, I'm afraid I can't hand in my paper on time. I've been sick for a week.
**M:** Well, I can give you an extension until Friday, but that's the absolute deadline.
**Q:** What will the woman probably do?
  `}
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="What will the woman probably do?"
  options={[
    "Submit the paper immediately",
    "Ask for another extension",
    "Submit the paper by Friday",
    "Drop the course"
  ]}
  answer="Submit the paper by Friday"
  explanation="关键词：extension until Friday（延期到周五）, absolute deadline（最后期限）。"
/>

### 2. 日常交际 Daily Communication

**高频词汇：**
- appointment（预约）、cancel（取消）、postpone（推迟）
- available（有空的）、busy（忙碌的）、convenient（方便的）
- invitation（邀请）、accept（接受）、decline（拒绝）

<AudioPlayer 
  src="/audio/listening/conversation-03.mp3"
  transcript={`
**M:** Would you like to go to the movies with me tonight?
**W:** I'd love to, but I have to prepare for tomorrow's presentation. How about this weekend?
**Q:** What does the woman mean?
  `}
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="What does the woman mean?"
  options={[
    "She doesn't like movies",
    "She's free tonight",
    "She suggests going this weekend",
    "She has already seen the movie"
  ]}
  answer="She suggests going this weekend"
  explanation="关键词：I'd love to, but...（委婉拒绝），How about this weekend?（建议周末）。"
/>

### 3. 服务场所 Service Places

**高频词汇：**
- restaurant（餐厅）、reservation（预订）、menu（菜单）
- hotel（酒店）、check in/out（入住/退房）、reception（前台）
- repair（维修）、fix（修理）、replace（更换）

<AudioPlayer 
  src="/audio/listening/conversation-04.mp3"
  transcript={`

**M:** No, we don't. But we'd like a table for four, please.
**W:** I'm afraid all tables are taken right now. There'll be a 20-minute wait.
**Q:** What does the woman mean?
  `}
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="What does the woman mean?"
  options={[
    "They can be seated immediately",
    "They need to wait for 20 minutes",
    "They must make a reservation",
    "The restaurant is closed"
  ]}
  answer="They need to wait for 20 minutes"
  explanation="关键词：all tables are taken（客满）, 20-minute wait（等20分钟）。"
/>
