## 词汇测验

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="Which word means '食欲；胃口'?"
  options={["recipe", "appetite", "ingredient", "portion"]}
  answer="appetite"
  explanation="appetite 意为'食欲、胃口'。常见搭配：lose one's appetite（没有食欲）、have a good appetite（胃口好）。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="The flight was _____ due to heavy fog."
  options={["departed", "delayed", "delivered", "declined"]}
  answer="delayed"
  explanation="delay 意为'延误、推迟'。be delayed 是被延误的意思，常用于航班、火车等交通工具。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="Keep the _____ in case you want a refund. (收据)"
  answer="receipt"
  explanation="receipt 意为'收据、小票'，常见搭配：keep the receipt（保留收据）。注意发音中 p 不发音。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="The doctor _____ some medicine for my cold. (开处方)"
  answer="prescribed"
  explanation="prescribe 意为'开处方'，过去式为 prescribed。常见搭配：prescribe medicine（开药）。"
/>
