## 词汇测验

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="The _____ is due next Monday."
  options={["assignment", "apartment", "appointment", "achievement"]}
  answer="assignment"
  explanation="assignment 意为'作业、任务'。be due 意为'到期'，整句意思是：作业下周一截止。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="Which word means '学期'?"
  options={["semester", "seminar", "ceremony", "summary"]}
  answer="semester"
  explanation="semester 意为'学期'（通常一学年分两个学期）。注意区分 seminar（研讨会）。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="She _____ in computer science at this university. (主修)"
  answer="majors"
  explanation="major in 意为'主修某专业'。主语 She 是第三人称单数，所以用 majors。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="Please _____ your paper before Friday. (提交)"
  answer="submit"
  explanation="submit 意为'提交'，常见搭配：submit a paper/report/application。"
/>
