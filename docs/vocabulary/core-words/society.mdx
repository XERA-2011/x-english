## 词汇测验

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="Smoking is _____ in public buildings."
  options={["promoted", "prohibited", "produced", "processed"]}
  answer="prohibited"
  explanation="prohibit 意为'禁止'。be prohibited = 被禁止。注意与 promote（促进）区分。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="Which word means '志愿者'?"
  options={["victim", "volunteer", "veteran", "violator"]}
  answer="volunteer"
  explanation="volunteer 意为'志愿者'，也可作动词表示'自愿做某事'。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="The world _____ is over 8 billion now. (人口)"
  answer="population"
  explanation="population 意为'人口'，常见搭配：population growth（人口增长）。"
/>
