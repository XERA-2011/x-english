## 词汇测验

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="She felt _____ about the upcoming interview."
  options={["anxious", "ambitious", "artificial", "abundant"]}
  answer="anxious"
  explanation="anxious 意为'焦虑的'，feel anxious about 表示'对……感到焦虑'。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="Which word means '可靠的'?"
  options={["reliable", "relative", "relevant", "reluctant"]}
  answer="reliable"
  explanation="reliable 意为'可靠的、可信赖的'。rely（依赖）+ able → reliable。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="He _____ for being late to the meeting. (道歉)"
  answer="apologized"
  explanation="apologize 意为'道歉'，常见搭配：apologize for（为……道歉）。"
/>
