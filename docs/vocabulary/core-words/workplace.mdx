## 词汇测验

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="She got a _____ and became the department manager."
  options={["pension", "promotion", "permission", "profession"]}
  answer="promotion"
  explanation="promotion 意为'晋升'，get a promotion 是'获得晋升'的意思。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="Which word means '简历'?"
  options={["reference", "resume", "receipt", "review"]}
  answer="resume"
  explanation="resume（也写作 résumé）意为'简历'，是求职时必备的文件。"
/>

<Quiz
  type="fill-blank"
  question="The _____ for this project is next Monday. (截止日期)"
  answer="deadline"
  explanation="deadline 意为'截止日期'，常见搭配：meet the deadline（赶上截止日期）。"
/>
