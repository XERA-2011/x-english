# Part B：图片描述与互动讨论

### 1) 常见提问（来自模板）

- What does the first/second/third/fourth picture show?
- What can you get from the four pictures?
- Which activity do you like best? And why?
- Which activity do you dislike? And why?
- What do you think of the activity in picture ...?

### 2) 图片题万能答题框架（30-45秒）

```text
The picture shows that ...
From my point of view, ...
The main reason is that ...
For example, ...
So I think ...
```

### 3) 实战演练（含图片示例）

在实际考试中，考官会给出具体的场景图片让你描述。以下提供四幅典型的 PET3 口试图片及参考回答。

#### 示例 1：户外运动 (Outdoor Sports)

<img src="/img/pet3/park_sports.png" alt="Outdoor Sports" width="400" />

**考官提问:** What do you think of the activity in this picture?
**参考回答:**
> **The picture shows that** some people are playing sports and relaxing in a sunny park. A woman is riding a bicycle, and others are jogging or having a picnic.
> **From my point of view,** this is a very healthy and enjoyable way to spend free time.
> **The main reason is that** exercising outdoors is good for our physical health and helps reduce stress.
> **For example,** after a long week of work or study, jogging in the fresh air makes me feel completely refreshed.
> **So I think** we should all spend more time doing outdoor activities.

#### 示例 2：图书馆学习 (Studying in the Library)

<img src="/img/pet3/library_study.png" alt="Library Study" width="400" />

**考官提问:** Which activity do you like best? And why? (Assuming this is one of the options)
**参考回答:**
> **The picture shows that** a group of students is studying together in a modern library. They are looking at books and using laptops.
> **From my point of view,** I like this activity best because group study is highly effective.
> **The main reason is that** we can share ideas and help each other when we meet difficult problems.
> **For example,** last week my friends and I prepared for a math exam together, and it was much easier than studying alone.
> **So I think** studying in a library with friends is an excellent choice for students.

#### 示例 3：超市购物 (Supermarket Shopping)

<img src="/img/pet3/supermarket_shopping.png" alt="Supermarket Shopping" width="400" />

**考官提问:** What can you get from this picture?
**参考回答:**
> **The picture shows that** people are shopping for groceries in a modern supermarket. One person is pushing a cart, and another is choosing fruits.
> **From my point of view,** shopping in a supermarket is very convenient for our daily lives.
> **The main reason is that** we can buy almost everything we need in one place.
> **For example,** I usually go to the supermarket with my parents on weekends to buy fresh vegetables, meat, and daily supplies.
> **So I think** supermarkets play a very important role in modern city life.

#### 示例 4：办公室工作 (Working in the Office)

<img src="/img/pet3/office_working.png" alt="Office Working" width="400" />

**考官提问:** What do you think of the activity in picture 4?
**参考回答:**
> **The picture shows that** some young professionals are working collaboratively in a modern office. Two people are discussing something while looking at a laptop screen.
> **From my point of view,** teamwork is essential in today's workplace.
> **The main reason is that** cooperating with others can help us solve problems more efficiently.
> **For example,** when I worked on a group project last month, discussing with my teammates gave me many new ideas that I couldn't think of alone.
> **So I think** developing good communication skills is very important for our future careers.

### 4) 听不清时的补救句

- I'm sorry. Could you please speak more slowly?
- Pardon? Could you repeat the question?
- Sorry, I didn't catch that. Could you say it again?
