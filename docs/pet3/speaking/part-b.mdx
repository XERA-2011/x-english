# Part B：考生间互动 (Collaborative Task)

在这一部分（约 4 分钟），两名考生将根据考官给出的**情景说明**和**提示**进行讨论任务。你们需要互相协作，通过询问、建议、表达同意或不同意，最终达成共识或完成任务。掌握一些常用的过渡句和互动句型非常重要，**切忌一个人自顾自地说**。

### 1) 开启话题与提出建议

- Shall we start with this one?
- Why don't we consider...?
- How about choosing...?
- I think it would be a good idea to...

### 2) 征求对方意见（高分互动关键）

- What do you think?
- Do you agree with me?
- How do you feel about this?

### 3) 表达同意并补充

- I completely agree with you. And what's more...
- You are right, and I would like to add that ...
- That's a great idea! Besides, we can also...
- What you said makes sense. In my opinion, ...

### 4) 委婉表达不同意

- I agree with you up to a point, but...
- That might be true, but shouldn't we also consider...?
- I can see your point, but I'm afraid I have a different view.
- I don't quite agree because ...

### 5) 结束讨论与达成共识

- So, have we reached an agreement?
- To sum up, we should choose...
- It seems we both agree that... is the best choice.

### 6) 实战演练 (Collaborative Task 示例)

以下提供一幅 PETS-3 典型的双人互动情景图，以及模拟两位考生的讨论过程。

#### 任务情景：计划一次班级周末出游 (Planning a Weekend Trip)

**考官提示:** "Here is a picture showing some travel brochures and a map. I'd like you to talk together about where you want to go for a weekend trip and what you want to do there. You have 4 minutes."

<img src="/img/pet3/trip_planning.png" alt="Trip Planning" width="400" />

**考生互动示范 (Candidate A & B):**

> **A:** **Shall we start by** looking at the map? It seems we have a few options for our weekend trip.
> **B:** **That's a good idea.** I see a brochure for a city tour, but I think going to the countryside might be more relaxing. **What do you think?**
> **A:** **I completely agree with you.** After a week of studying, fresh air would be great. **How about choosing** the mountain area? We could go hiking.
> **B:** **You are right, and I would like to add that** we can also have a picnic at the top of the mountain. But is everyone in our class good at climbing?
> **A:** **That might be true, but shouldn't we also consider** renting a bus? That way, even if some students get tired, they can rest in the bus.
> **B:** **What you said makes sense.** We can prepare some snacks and drinks, too.
> **A:** Perfect. **So, have we reached an agreement?** We will go hiking in the mountains and rent a bus for the trip.
> **B:** Yes! **It seems we both agree that** a mountain hiking trip with a picnic is the best choice.
>
> ---
> **A:** **我们要不先从**看地图开始？看来我们周末的旅行有几个选择。
> **B:** **好主意。** 我看到一本城市旅游的手册，但我认为去乡下可能会更让人放松。**你觉得呢？**
> **A:** **我完全同意。** 学习了一周后，呼吸点新鲜空气会很棒。**选择**去山区怎么样？我们可以去远足。
> **B:** **你说得对，我还想补充的是**我们也可以在山顶野餐。但是班上每个人都擅长爬山吗？
> **A:** **也许确实如此，但我们是不是也该考虑**租一辆大巴？这样的话，即使有些同学累了，他们也可以在车上休息。
> **B:** **你说的有道理。** 我们也可以准备一些零食和饮料。
> **A:** 完美。**那么，我们达成共识了吗？** 我们将去山里远足，并租一辆大巴去旅行。
> **B:** 是的！**看来我们都同意**带野餐的山区远足旅行是最好的选择。

#### 任务情景：为老师挑选离别礼物 (Choosing a Farewell Gift)

**考官提示:** "Here are some pictures showing different gift options. Your favorite English teacher is leaving the school soon. I'd like you to talk together about what to buy as a farewell gift. You have 4 minutes."

<img src="/img/pet3/teacher_gift.png" alt="Choosing a Gift" width="400" />

**考生互动示范 (Candidate A & B):**

> **A:** Our English teacher has helped us a lot. **Why don't we consider** buying a nice fountain pen? It's very useful for a teacher.
> **B:** **That might be true, but shouldn't we also consider** the price? A good fountain pen could be quite expensive. **How about choosing** a classic book? He loves reading.
> **A:** **That's a great idea!** A book is meaningful and affordable. **Besides, we can also** write some thank-you notes on the first page.
> **B:** **I completely agree with you.** But a book alone might look a bit simple. **What do you think** about adding a bouquet of flowers?
> **A:** **You are right, and I would like to add that** flowers can show our respect and best wishes.
> **B:** Excellent. **So, have we reached an agreement?**
> **A:** Yes. **It seems we both agree that** a classic book with our notes and a beautiful bouquet of flowers will be the perfect gift.
>
> ---
> **A:** 我们的英语老师帮了我们很多。**为什么我们不考虑**买一支好的钢笔呢？这对老师来说非常实用。
> **B:** **也许确实如此，但我们是不是也该考虑**一下价格？一支好的钢笔可能相当昂贵。**选择**一本经典书籍怎么样？他很喜欢阅读。
> **A:** **真是个好主意！** 书籍既有意义又买得起。**此外，我们也可以**在第一页写一些感谢的话。
> **B:** **我完全同意。** 但是仅仅一本书可能看起来有点单调。**你觉得**加一束花怎么样？
> **A:** **你说得对，我还想补充的是**鲜花可以表达我们的敬意和美好的祝愿。
> **B:** 太好了。**那么，我们达成共识了吗？**
> **A:** 是的。**看来我们都同意**一本带有我们留言的经典书籍和一束美丽的鲜花将是完美的礼物。

#### 任务情景：讨论如何提高英语口语 (Improving English Speaking)

**考官提示:** "Here is a picture showing different ways to learn English. Many students find speaking English difficult. I'd like you to talk together about the best ways to improve English speaking skills. You have 4 minutes."

<img src="/img/pet3/improve_english.png" alt="Improving English" width="400" />

**考生互动示范 (Candidate A & B):**

> **A:** **Shall we start with** using language apps? I can see a phone with an app in the picture. It's very convenient to practice pronunciation every day.
> **B:** **What you said makes sense.** Apps are good for beginners. **However, I agree with you up to a point, but** practicing with real people is more important for speaking. **How do you feel about** joining an online class?
> **A:** **That's a good idea.** In an online class, we can talk to teachers and get immediate feedback. **What do you think** about reading textbooks?
> **B:** **I can see your point, but I'm afraid I have a different view.** Textbooks are great for grammar, but not very helpful for speaking. I think joining an English corner or talking to foreigners is better.
> **A:** **I completely agree with you.** Real conversations are the key. **To sum up, we should choose** online classes and real-life practice over just reading books.
> **B:** Exactly! **It seems we both agree that** interacting with people is the most effective way to improve spoken English.
>
> ---
> **【中文翻译】**
> **A:** **我们要不先从**使用语言应用开始？我能在图中看到一部打开了App的手机。每天练习发音非常方便。
> **B:** **你说的有道理。** 应用对初学者很好。**然而，我在某种程度上同意你，但是**和真人练习对口语更重要。**你对**参加在线课程**有什么看法**？
> **A:** **好主意。** 在在线课程中，我们可以和老师交谈并得到即时反馈。**你觉得**阅读教科书怎么样？
> **B:** **我明白你的观点，但恐怕我有不同的看法。** 教科书对语法很好，但对口语帮助不大。我认为参加英语角或和外国人交谈更好。
> **A:** **我完全同意。** 真实的对话是关键。**总而言之，我们应该选择**在线课程和真实的练习，而不是仅仅看书。
> **B:** 没错！**看来我们都同意**与人互动是提高英语口语最有效的方法。
