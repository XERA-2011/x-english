# Part C：个人长谈与图片描述 (Individual Talk)

在这一部分（约 3 分钟），考官会分别给两名考生一张图片。你需要根据图片进行 1.5 分钟左右的**连续性表达**。之后，考官会针对该话题向你的搭档提一个简单问题。这部分重点考察你的语言组织能力和连贯性。

### 1) 常见提问方式

- What does the picture show?
- What do you think of the activity/phenomenon in the picture?
- (对搭档) Do you agree with him/her? / What's your opinion?

### 2) 图片长谈万能答题框架（1.5分钟）

```text
(1. 描述图片核心内容) The picture shows that ...
(2. 表达核心观点) From my point of view, ...
(3. 阐述原因) The main reason is that ... / First of all, ...
(4. 举例说明) For example, ...
(5. 总结升华) So I think / Therefore, ...
```

### 3) 实战演练（含图片示例）

以下提供四幅典型的 PETS-3 口试图片及参考的个人长谈回答。

#### 示例 1：户外运动 (Outdoor Sports)

<img src="/img/pet3/park_sports.png" alt="Outdoor Sports" width="400" />

**考官提问:** What do you think of the activity in this picture?
**参考回答 (1.5 min):**
> **The picture shows that** some people are playing sports and relaxing in a sunny park. A woman is riding a bicycle, and others are jogging or having a picnic.
> **From my point of view,** this is a very healthy and enjoyable way to spend free time.
> **The main reason is that** exercising outdoors is good for our physical health and helps reduce stress. Modern people often sit in offices all day, so going outside is extremely necessary.
> **For example,** after a long week of work or study, jogging in the fresh air makes me feel completely refreshed and energetic again.
> **So I think** we should all balance our work and life, and spend more time doing outdoor activities.
>
> ---
> **图片显示**一些人正在阳光明媚的公园里做运动和放松。一位女士在骑自行车，其他人正在慢跑或野餐。
> **在我看来，**这是一种非常健康和愉快的度过闲暇时光的方式。
> **主要原因是**户外运动有益于我们的身体健康并有助于减轻压力。现代人经常整天坐在办公室里，所以走到户外是非常必要的。
> **例如，**在经过漫长一周的工作或学习后，在新鲜空气中慢跑让我感到完全焕然一新，重新充满活力。
> **因此我认为**我们都应该平衡工作与生活，花更多时间进行户外活动。

#### 示例 2：图书馆学习 (Studying in the Library)

<img src="/img/pet3/library_study.png" alt="Library Study" width="400" />

**考官提问:** What can you tell us about this picture?
**参考回答 (1.5 min):**
> **The picture shows that** a group of students is studying together in a modern library. They are looking at books and using laptops.
> **From my point of view,** group study is a highly effective way of learning.
> **The main reason is that** we can share ideas and help each other when we meet difficult problems. It is much faster than figuring everything out alone.
> **For example,** last week my friends and I prepared for a math exam together. We explained concepts to each other, and it was much easier than studying by myself.
> **So I think** studying in a library with friends is an excellent choice for college students.
>
> ---
> **图片显示**一群学生正在现代化的图书馆里一起学习。他们正在看书和使用笔记本电脑。
> **在我看来，**小组学习是一种非常高效的学习方式。
> **主要原因是**当我们遇到难题时，我们可以分享想法并互相帮助。这比独自想通所有事情要快得多。
> **例如，**上周我和朋友们一起准备数学考试。我们互相解释概念，这比我自己学习要容易得多。
> **因此我认为**和朋友一起在图书馆学习对大学生来说是一个极好的选择。

#### 示例 3：超市购物 (Supermarket Shopping)

<img src="/img/pet3/supermarket_shopping.png" alt="Supermarket Shopping" width="400" />

**考官提问:** What do you think of the phenomenon in this picture?
**参考回答 (1.5 min):**
> **The picture shows that** people are shopping for groceries in a modern supermarket. One person is pushing a cart, and another is choosing fruits.
> **From my point of view,** shopping in a supermarket is very convenient for our daily lives.
> **The main reason is that** we can buy almost everything we need in one place, from fresh food to daily necessities. Also, the quality is usually guaranteed.
> **For example,** I usually go to the supermarket with my parents on weekends to buy fresh vegetables, meat, and daily supplies for the whole week. It saves us a lot of time.
> **So I think** supermarkets, and even online grocery shopping, play a very important role in modern city life.
>
> ---
> **图片显示**人们正在现代化的超市里购买食品杂货。一个人推着购物车，另一个人在挑选水果。
> **在我看来，**在超市购物对我们的日常生活非常方便。
> **主要原因是**我们可以在一个地方买到几乎所有我们需要的东西，从新鲜食品到日用品。而且，质量通常有保证。
> **例如，**我通常在周末和父母去超市购买全周的新鲜蔬菜、肉类和日用品。这节省了我们很多时间。
> **因此我认为**超市，甚至在线杂货购物，在现代城市生活中发挥着非常重要的作用。

#### 示例 4：办公室工作 (Working in the Office)

<img src="/img/pet3/office_working.png" alt="Office Working" width="400" />

**考官提问:** What is your opinion on the activity shown in picture 4?
**参考回答 (1.5 min):**
> **The picture shows that** some young professionals are working collaboratively in a modern office. Two people are discussing something while looking at a laptop screen.
> **From my point of view,** teamwork is essential in today's workplace.
> **The main reason is that** cooperating with others can help us solve complex problems more efficiently. Everyone has different strengths and perspectives.
> **For example,** when I worked on a group project last month, discussing with my teammates gave me many new ideas that I couldn't think of alone.
> **So I think** developing good communication and teamwork skills is very important for our future careers.
>
> ---
> **图片显示**一些年轻的专业人士正在现代化的办公室里协同工作。两个人看着笔记本电脑屏幕在讨论一些事情。
> **在我看来，**团队合作在当今的工作场所至关重要。
> **主要原因是**与他人合作可以帮助我们更高效地解决复杂问题。每个人都有不同的优势和视角。
> **例如，**上个月我做小组项目时，和队友的讨论给了我很多我自己想不到的新想法。
> **因此我认为**培养良好的沟通和团队合作能力对我们未来的职业生涯非常重要。

### 4) 听不清时的补救句 (适用于听考官提问或搭档提问时)

- I'm sorry. Could you please speak more slowly?
- Pardon? Could you repeat the question?
- Sorry, I didn't catch that. Could you say it again?
