# PET3 写作指南整理 <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

> 全国英语等级考试第三级（PETS-3）写作主要分为两部分：**A节（应用文）**和**B节（短文写作）**。本页面全面覆盖了 PET3 考试中可能出现的所有高频题型和经典范文。

## A节：应用性写作（10分，约100词）

A节要求写一封书信或一则便条/通知。题目会给出 **3-5 个交际任务点**，每个任务点都必须覆盖，不能遗漏，否则直接扣内容分。格式要正确（称呼、署名不可缺），语言表达要得体。

<details>
<summary><b>Apology 道歉信</b></summary>
<br/>

```text
Dear ______,

I am writing to apologize for ______（道歉原因）.
I am terribly sorry for the inconvenience this has caused you.
I hope you can understand my situation and accept my sincere apology.

Please allow me to make up for this matter by ______（补救措施）.
Thank you for your understanding.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

尊敬的______：

我写信是为了就______（道歉原因）向您道歉。
对此给您带来的不便，我深感抱歉。
希望您能理解我的处境，并接受我诚挚的歉意。

请允许我通过______（补救措施）来弥补这件事。
感谢您的理解。

此致
敬礼
李明
```

<details>
<summary><b>Note 便条</b></summary>
<br/>

```text
To: ______（收件人姓名）
From: ______（发件人姓名）
Date: ______（如：Monday, April 21, 2026）

I am writing this note to let you know that ______（核心事由）.

I will not be available / back until ______（可联系/归来时间）.
In the meantime, could you please ______（请对方协助的具体事项）?
I would really appreciate your help.

I have already ______（已处理的事项，如：left the key on your desk /
called to cancel the appointment）. If anything urgent comes up,
please feel free to reach me at ______（联系电话 / 邮箱）.

Sorry for any inconvenience this may cause.

致：______（收件人姓名）
发件人：______（发件人姓名）
日期：______

我留此便条，通知您______（核心事由）。

我要到______（时间）才能回来/可联系。在此期间，能否麻烦您
______（请对方协助的具体事项）？非常感谢您的帮助。

我已经______（已处理的事项）。如有紧急情况，请拨打______
（联系电话/邮箱）与我联系。

对此给您带来的不便，深表歉意。
```

**便条常用句型速查**

| 功能 | 常用英语表达 |
|---|---|
| 开头说明事由 | I am writing this note to let you know that... / Just to inform you that... |
| 说明去向/不在原因 | I have gone to ______ and will be back by ______. / I will be out of the office until ______. |
| 请对方代劳 | Could you please pass this message to ______? / Would you mind helping me ______? |
| 留下物品告知 | I have left ______ on your desk. / Please find ______ in ______. |
| 请勿忘记提醒 | Please don't forget to ______. / Please remember to ______. |
| 结尾致歉/致谢 | Sorry for any inconvenience. / Thank you for your understanding and cooperation. |

> 💡 **格式要点**：无需 Dear / Yours sincerely；直接写 **To / From / Date**，正文 3-5 句话，简洁说明事项即可。

</details>

```text
will be held on ______（日期） at ______（地点）.

The event / activity will start at ______（开始时间） and end at ______（结束时间）.
______（活动内容简介，1-2句，如：Participants will have the opportunity to ______）.

Those who are interested are invited to sign up at ______（报名地点）
before ______（报名截止日期）. Please note that ______（注意事项，如：
all participants should bring their student ID cards /
attendance is compulsory for all members）.

We look forward to your active participation.
For further information, please do not hesitate to contact ______（联系人及联系方式）.

                                             ______（落款单位）
                                             ______（日期）

                           通   知

______（发出通知的组织/单位）兹宣布，______（活动名称/事项）
将于______（日期）在______（地点）举行。

活动将于______（开始时间）开始，______（结束时间）结束。
______（活动内容简介，如：参与者将有机会……）。

有意参加者请于______（截止日期）前在______（报名地点）报名。
请注意，______（注意事项，如：参与者须携带学生证 / 全体成员须强制参加）。

欢迎积极参与。如需进一步了解，请联系______（联系人及联系方式）。

                                             ______（落款单位）
                                             ______（日期）
```

**通知常用句型速查**

| 功能 | 常用英语表达 |
|---|---|
| 开头宣布活动 | ______ is pleased to announce that... / We are delighted to inform you that... |
| 说明时间地点 | The event will be held on ______ at ______. / It is scheduled for ______ in ______. |
| 活动内容说明 | Participants will have the opportunity to ______. / The program will include ______. |
| 号召报名参与 | Those interested are invited to sign up at ______ before ______. / Anyone who wishes to participate should ______. |
| 注意事项 | Please note that ______. / Participants are required to ______. / Please bring ______ with you. |
| 结尾鼓励参与 | We look forward to your active participation. / Your presence would be greatly appreciated. |
| 联系方式说明 | For further information, please contact ______ at ______. / Should you have any questions, please feel free to reach us at ______. |

> 💡 **格式要点**：标题 **NOTICE** 居中大写；落款靠右；正文用**第三人称**；无需称呼与个人署名。

## B节：控制性写作（20分，120-150词）

B节通常要求根据所给情景、图表或提纲写一篇议论文或说明文。文章结构要清晰（通常为**三段论**），逻辑严密。最常见的类型是：**现象评论/看图作文**、**观点论述**、**图表描述**。

### 1. 现象评论/看图作文

**题目要求：** 描述年轻人喜欢点外卖（ordering food online）而不是自己做饭的现象，分析原因，并给出你的看法。

:::info[📖 范文]
Nowadays, ordering food online has become increasingly popular among young people. <strong className="text--primary">It is a common phenomenon that</strong> many office workers and students prefer food delivery to cooking by themselves.

<strong className="text--primary">There are two main reasons for this trend. First of all,</strong> the fast pace of modern life puts young people under great pressure. After a busy day, they are often too tired to cook. <strong className="text--primary">Furthermore,</strong> food delivery apps offer various choices and great convenience.

<strong className="text--primary">In my opinion, while</strong> ordering food online brings convenience, <strong className="text--primary">we should not ignore its disadvantages, such as</strong> food safety issues. <strong className="text--primary">Therefore, it is advisable to</strong> cook for ourselves when we have free time.
:::

### 2. 观点论述题

**题目要求：** 有人认为金钱是生活中最重要的东西，有人则不同意。写一篇短文表明你的观点并给出理由。

:::info[📖 范文]
<strong className="text--primary">When it comes to</strong> the importance of money, <strong className="text--primary">people hold different views. Some believe</strong> money is the most important thing in life, <strong className="text--primary">while others disagree. From my perspective,</strong> money is necessary, but it is not everything.

<strong className="text--primary">On the one hand,</strong> we need money to survive. It provides us with basic needs such as food, housing, and education. <strong className="text--primary">On the other hand,</strong> there are many precious things that money cannot buy, such as true friendship, good health, and true happiness. A wealthy person may suffer from illness or loneliness.

<strong className="text--primary">To sum up, we should</strong> work hard to earn a living, <strong className="text--primary">but we must never</strong> become slaves to money. <strong className="text--primary">We should cherish the things that truly matter in life.</strong>
:::

### 3. 图表描述题

**题目要求：** 描述过去几年人们出行方式的变化（私家车增多，自行车减少），分析原因及影响。

:::info[📖 范文]
<strong className="text--primary">As is clearly shown in the chart, there has been a dramatic change in</strong> people's ways of traveling over the past few years. The proportion of people driving private cars has increased rapidly, while the number of people riding bicycles has decreased significantly.

<strong className="text--primary">Several factors account for this change. First,</strong> with the improvement of living standards, more and more families can afford to buy cars. <strong className="text--primary">Second,</strong> driving is much faster and more comfortable than riding a bike, especially in bad weather. 

<strong className="text--primary">However,</strong> the increase in cars has caused serious problems such as traffic jams and air pollution. <strong className="text--primary">Therefore, the government should</strong> encourage citizens to use public transportation or ride bicycles to protect the environment.
:::

## 📊 评分标准

考官从以下三个维度打分，缺一不可：

| 维度 | 说明 | 高分要点 |
|---|---|---|
| **内容完整性** | 任务点全部覆盖 | Section A 逐条对应题目给出的 3-5 个交际任务；Section B 三段结构完整 |
| **语言准确性** | 减少拼写与语法错误 | 重点保证**主谓一致、时态一致**；避免中式英语 |
| **连贯性** | 逻辑层次清晰 | 熟练使用连接词：*Firstly / In addition / However / Consequently / To sum up* |

---

## 🎯 备考建议

:::tip 高分策略
1. **背诵功能句型**：Section A 的万能开头句（如 *I am writing to express my gratitude for...*）每类至少背一句
2. **控制作答时间**：建议 Section A 用 **15 分钟**，Section B 用 **25 分钟**，留 5 分钟检查
3. **字数不要超**：Section B 写到 140 词左右即可，写太多容易因赶时间出现低级错误
4. **卷面整洁**：2026 年以人工阅卷为主，字迹清晰能留下良好印象，涂改不超过 3 处
5. **任务点先列再写**：拿到题目后，先在草稿纸上列出所有任务点，逐一打勾，确保无遗漏
:::
