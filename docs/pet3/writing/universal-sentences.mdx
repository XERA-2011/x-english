## 写作万能句型宝典

这部分为您精选了可以在 PETS-3 写作（尤其是 B 节大作文）中直接套用的高分结构。建议挑选您觉得最顺口的几句牢固背诵即可。

### 🚀 高分逻辑语料库

<details open>
<summary><b>1. 现象引入 / 原因剖析 (用于段首或过渡)</b></summary>
<br/>

| 英文经典表达 | 中文释义 |
| :--- | :--- |
| **There are probably three/many/several reasons for this dramatic increase/decline in…** | 引起……显著增长（下降）的原因有许多。 |
| **Some reasons can explain this trend. First of all,… Secondly,… Furthermore,…** | 一些原因可以解释这一趋势。首先……其次……另外…… |
| **The causes for…are varied. They include…Perhaps the main cause is…** | 造成……的原因有很多，包括……主要的原因可能是…… |
| **It is no easy task to identify the reasons for this phenomenon which involves several complicated factors.** | 要找出这一现象的原因并非易事，因为它涉及若干复杂的因素。|
| **Certainly,…is not the sole reason for…; …is also responsible for the change/problem.** | 当然，……并不是唯一的原因。还有……也导致了这一变化。|
| **There are different opinions among people as to ____ .Some people suggest that ____.** | 关于……人们有不同的观点。一些人认为……|
| **There is an old saying ______. It’s the experience of our forefathers, however, it is correct in many cases even today.** | 俗话说（常言道）……，它是前辈的经验，但即使在今天依旧适用。|
| **Today, ____, which have brought a lot of harm in our daily life. First, ____ Second, ____. What makes things worse is that ____.** | 现在，……给我们的日常生活带来了许多危害。首先……其次……更为糟糕的是……|
| **Everything has two sides and ______ is not an exception, it has both advantages and disadvantages.** | 任何事物都有两面性，……也不例外。它既有有利的一面，也有不利的一面。|
| **Man is now facing a big problem ______ which is becoming more and more serious.** | 人类正面临着一个严重的问题……，这个问题变得越来越严重。|

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>2. 观点冲突 / 对比 / 措施 (用于中间段落展开)</b></summary>
<br/>

| 英文经典表达 | 中文释义 |
| :--- | :--- |
| **On the contrary, there are some people in favor of ___. At the same time, they say____.** | 相反，有一些人赞成……，他们相信……，而且他们认为……|
| **But I don’t think it is a very good way to solve ____. For example, ____. Worst of all, ___.** | 但是，我认为这不是解决……的好方法，比如……最糟糕的是……|
| **Confronted with ______, we should take a series of effective measures to ______. For one thing, ______ For another, ______** | 面临……，我们应该采取一系列行之有效的方法来……一方面……另一方面……|
| **It is high time that something was done about it. For example, _____. In addition, _____. All these measures will certainly ______** | 早就应该拿出行动了。比如说……，另外……所有这些方法肯定会……|
| **However, just like everything has both its good and bad sides, ______ also has its own disadvantages, such as ______.** | 然而，正如任何事物都有两面一样，……也有它的不利一面，像……|
| **I fully agree with the statement that ______ because ______.** | 我完全同意……这种观点，主要理由如下……|

</details>


<details>
<summary><b>3. 总结归纳 / 升华立意 (用于结尾段)</b></summary>
<br/>

| 英文经典表达 | 中文释义 |
| :--- | :--- |
| **As far as I am concerned, I agree with the latter opinion to some extent. I think that …** | 至于我，在某种程度上我同意后面的观点，我认为…… |
| **In a word, the whole society should pay close attention to the problem of … Only in this way can … in the future.** | 总而言之，全社会应该密切关注……这个问题。只有这样，我们才能在将来…… |
| **Personally, I believe that… Consequently, I’m confident that a bright future is awaiting us because…** | 就我个人而言，我相信……因此，坚信美好的未来正等着我们，因为…… |
| **With the development of society, … So its urgent and necessary to …** | 随着社会的发展，……因此，迫切需要…… |
| **For my part, I think it reasonable to… Only in this way can you…** | 就我而言，我认为……更合理。只有这样，我们才能…… |
| **In my opinion, I think it necessary to… The reasons are as follows. First … second … Last but not least, …** | 对我来说，我认为有必要……原因如下：第一……；第二……；最后但同样重要的是…… |
| **From what has been discussed above, we may reasonably arrive at the conclusion that…** | 综上所述，我们可以清楚地得出结论…… |

</details>
