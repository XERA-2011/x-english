# PET3 口试指导 <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

> 根据你提供的 `PETS三级口试模板.pdf` 整理，按口试三部分给出可直接套用的答题模板。

## 一、Part A：个人信息问答（自我表达）

### 1) Hometown（家乡）

```text
T: Do you live near here?
S: Yes, I do. / No, I don't. I live in + 居住地.

T: Where do you live?
S: I live in + 城市/地区.

T: What do you think are the good points about living there?
S: The weather is comfortable and people are kind.
   Most importantly, many of my friends are there.

T: How do you usually go to work/school? Why?
S: I often go by bus/train because it's cheap and convenient.
```

### 2) Leisure（业余时间）

```text
T: Do you have any hobbies?
S: Yes. I like reading / playing basketball / playing computer games.

T: What do you usually do in your spare time?
S: I often go shopping with friends.
   I often watch movies on my phone.
   I often travel to other cities.
```

### 3) Family（家庭）

```text
T: Can you tell us something about your family?
S: Of course. There are ... people in my family: ...

T: What does your family usually do at weekends?
S: We often stay at home watching TV.
   Sometimes we go out for a meal.

T: Do you enjoy it? Why?
S: Yes. We care for each other and understand each other.
```

### 4) Work（工作）

```text
T: Can you tell me something about your job?
S: I have worked for ... years. I like my job because ...

T: What do you enjoy most about your job?
S: The best part is that it helps me improve my abilities.

T: What do you dislike about your work?
S: I live far away from my company, so the commute is long.

T: Do you have any future plans?
S: Yes. I want to be promoted within two years.
```

### 5) Study（学习）

```text
T: Can you tell me something about your study?
S: I major in ... I have to take many courses such as ...

T: What do you enjoy most about your school?
S: The campus is beautiful and the teachers are friendly.

T: What do you dislike about your school?
S: There are too many ... which may distract students.

T: Do you have any future plans?
S: I want to be a ... and I am preparing for it now.
```

## 二、Part B：图片描述与互动讨论

### 1) 常见提问（来自模板）

- What does the first/second/third/fourth picture show?
- What can you get from the four pictures?
- Which activity do you like best? And why?
- Which activity do you dislike? And why?
- What do you think of the activity in picture ...?

### 2) 图片题万能答题框架（30-45秒）

```text
The picture shows that ...
From my point of view, ...
The main reason is that ...
For example, ...
So I think ...
```

### 3) 听不清时的补救句

- I'm sorry. Could you please speak more slowly?
- Pardon? Could you repeat the question?
- Sorry, I didn't catch that. Could you say it again?

## 三、Part C：讨论表达句（主陈述 + 回应）

### 1) 主陈述者常用句

- From the picture, we can see that ...
- It shows the phenomenon that ...
- In my opinion, the reason is that ...
- However, as far as I am concerned, ...
- Because ...

### 2) 次陈述者回应句

- I agree with you, but ...
- You are right, and I would like to add that ...
- What you said makes sense. In my opinion, ...
- I can't agree with you any more. / I don't quite agree because ...

### 3) 结束讨论句

- Different people have different opinions.
- To sum up, we should consider both sides.
- So a balanced view is the best choice.

## 四、口试临场策略（简明版）

1. 不追求高级词，优先“完整句 + 清晰逻辑”
2. 每次回答至少包含：观点 + 原因 + 例子
3. 遇到卡壳先用过渡句占位：`Well, in my opinion...`
4. 发音上优先保证关键词清楚，不要抢语速
