## 📖 实战阅读文本

*请利用“骨架阅读法”快速扫读，并标出各段的中心意图：*

Remote work has transformed the modern workplace, allowing employees to perform their duties from almost anywhere. While this trend offers great flexibility to individuals, it is also reshaping urban areas in unexpected ways.

First of all, many major cities are experiencing a decline in office space demand. As companies allow their staff to work from home, they no longer need large, expensive offices in city centers. Consequently, commercial real estate values have dropped significantly in downtown districts.

Moreover, the shift is affecting local businesses that rely heavily on office workers. Restaurants, coffee shops, and dry cleaners in business districts have reported a severe drop in sales. Some small shops have even been forced to close down permanently due to the lack of daily customers.

On the other hand, suburban and rural communities are seeing a positive impact. As workers move away from crowded city centers in search of larger, more affordable homes, local economies in smaller towns are starting to **flourish**. In conclusion, while remote work provides obvious benefits to individual workers, it poses a severe challenge to the traditional economic structure of large cities.

## ✍️ 交互式真题自测

### Q1: 事实细节题（细节定位）
<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="1. According to the passage, what is a direct consequence of companies allowing employees to work from home?"
  options={[
    "A rise in commercial real estate prices in downtown areas.",
    "An increase in suburban traffic jams.",
    "A decline in the demand for large city center offices.",
    "An immediate growth in downtown restaurant sales."
  ]}
  answer="A decline in the demand for large city center offices."
  explanation="【解析】
通过题干关键词 `companies allowing employees to work from home` 锁定在文章第二段：
'As companies allow their staff to work from home, they no longer need large, expensive offices in city centers.'（随着公司允许员工居家办公，他们不再需要市中心大型且昂贵的办公室了）。
这与选项 C 'A decline in the demand for large city center offices' (市中心大办公室需求下降) 在语义上完全一致。
选项 A 和 D 与原文所述的 'dropped significantly' 和 'drop in sales' 完全相反（属于反向干扰），选项 B 在文中并未提及（无中生有）。"
/>

### Q2: 词义猜测题（上下文推断）
<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="2. The word 'flourish' in paragraph 4 is closest in meaning to ______."
  options={[
    "decline",
    "struggle",
    "grow successfully",
    "change slowly"
  ]}
  answer="grow successfully"
  explanation="【解析】
定位到第四段目标句：'As workers move away from crowded city centers... local economies in smaller towns are starting to flourish.'
通过上下文的逻辑关系分析：'As workers move away...'（随着员工搬入郊区小镇寻找更大更便宜的房子，导致小镇人口增加，消费水平上升），因此对小镇的本地经济起到了“积极影响” (positive impact)。
由此可以推断，小镇的经济开始“繁荣发展、成功增长”。
四个选项中：`decline` (衰退)、`struggle` (挣扎) 均为消极词；`change slowly` (缓慢改变) 过于中性；唯有 `grow successfully` (成功增长/繁荣) 完美表达了 `flourish` (繁荣) 的意思。"
/>

### Q3: 逻辑推理题（言外之意）
<Quiz
  type="multiple-choice"
  question="3. What can be inferred from the passage about the impact of remote work?"
  options={[
    "It will eventually be banned by most companies in the near future.",
    "It redistributes economic activity from downtown districts to smaller communities.",
    "It has a completely negative effect on all local businesses.",
    "It benefits large city centers and suburban towns equally."
  ]}
  answer="It redistributes economic activity from downtown districts to smaller communities."
  explanation="【解析】
本题属于推理判断题。我们需要从全文各部分的对比中得出合理的结论。
第三段提到：市中心的商业区由于居家办公，本地商铺（餐厅、咖啡店）销售额暴跌甚至倒闭（市中心经济下滑）。
第四段提到：郊区和农村社区看到积极影响，随着人口迁入，小镇的本地经济开始繁荣（小镇经济上升）。
将两个事实结合进行合理推理：远程办公**将经济活动从市中心商业区重新分配/转移到了更小规模的郊区社区**。这正是选项 B 'It redistributes economic activity from downtown districts to smaller communities' 所表达的含义。
选项 A 属于无中生有；选项 C 'completely negative' 过于绝对且与第四段事实相反；选项 D 与原文中“市中心受挫、小镇繁荣”的对比不符。"
/>
