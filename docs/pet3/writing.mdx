# PET3 写作指南整理 <DifficultyBadge level="B1" />

> 全国英语等级考试第三级（PETS-3）写作主要分为两部分：**A节（应用文）**和**B节（短文写作）**。本页面全面覆盖了 PET3 考试中可能出现的所有高频题型和经典范文。

## A节：应用文万能模板 (约100词)

A节要求写一封书信（道歉信、咨询信、建议信、邀请信、投诉信等）或一则通知/便条。重点在于**格式准确、目的明确、语言得体**。以下是历届考试高频信件类型的通用模板：

## B节：短文写作 (约120词)

B节通常要求根据所给情景、图表或提纲写一篇议论文或说明文。最常见的类型是：**现象评论**、**观点论述**、**图表描述**。

### 1. 现象评论/看图作文

**题目要求：** 描述年轻人喜欢点外卖（ordering food online）而不是自己做饭的现象，分析原因，并给出你的看法。

:::info[📖 范文]
Nowadays, ordering food online has become increasingly popular among young people. <strong className="text--primary">It is a common phenomenon that</strong> many office workers and students prefer food delivery to cooking by themselves.

<strong className="text--primary">There are two main reasons for this trend. First of all,</strong> the fast pace of modern life puts young people under great pressure. After a busy day, they are often too tired to cook. <strong className="text--primary">Furthermore,</strong> food delivery apps offer various choices and great convenience.

<strong className="text--primary">In my opinion, while</strong> ordering food online brings convenience, <strong className="text--primary">we should not ignore its disadvantages, such as</strong> food safety issues. <strong className="text--primary">Therefore, it is advisable to</strong> cook for ourselves when we have free time.
:::

### 2. 观点论述题

**题目要求：** 有人认为金钱是生活中最重要的东西，有人则不同意。写一篇短文表明你的观点并给出理由。

:::info[📖 范文]
<strong className="text--primary">When it comes to</strong> the importance of money, <strong className="text--primary">people hold different views. Some believe</strong> money is the most important thing in life, <strong className="text--primary">while others disagree. From my perspective,</strong> money is necessary, but it is not everything.

<strong className="text--primary">On the one hand,</strong> we need money to survive. It provides us with basic needs such as food, housing, and education. <strong className="text--primary">On the other hand,</strong> there are many precious things that money cannot buy, such as true friendship, good health, and true happiness. A wealthy person may suffer from illness or loneliness.

<strong className="text--primary">To sum up, we should</strong> work hard to earn a living, <strong className="text--primary">but we must never</strong> become slaves to money. <strong className="text--primary">We should cherish the things that truly matter in life.</strong>
:::

### 3. 图表描述题

**题目要求：** 描述过去几年人们出行方式的变化（私家车增多，自行车减少），分析原因及影响。

:::info[📖 范文]
<strong className="text--primary">As is clearly shown in the chart, there has been a dramatic change in</strong> people's ways of traveling over the past few years. The proportion of people driving private cars has increased rapidly, while the number of people riding bicycles has decreased significantly.

<strong className="text--primary">Several factors account for this change. First,</strong> with the improvement of living standards, more and more families can afford to buy cars. <strong className="text--primary">Second,</strong> driving is much faster and more comfortable than riding a bike, especially in bad weather. 

<strong className="text--primary">However,</strong> the increase in cars has caused serious problems such as traffic jams and air pollution. <strong className="text--primary">Therefore, the government should</strong> encourage citizens to use public transportation or ride bicycles to protect the environment.
:::
